### Answer to Question 35

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-4/Chapter-27/Section-A/Q-35

- Answer:

$\frac{-4}{3}$

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Given limit $=\lim _{x \rightarrow 2}\left\{\frac{3^{x}+\frac{3^{3}}{3^{x}}-12}{\frac{3^{3}}{3^{x}}-3^{x / 2}}\right\}=\lim _{t \rightarrow 3}\left(\frac{t^{2}+\frac{27}{t^{2}}-12}{\frac{27}{t^{2}}-t}\right) \quad \text{[put } 3^{x/2}=t \text{ ]}$
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & =\lim _{t \rightarrow 3}\left(\frac{t^{4}-12 t^{2}+27}{27-t^{3}}\right)=\lim _{t \rightarrow 3} \frac{\left(t^{2}-3\right)(t+3)(t-3)}{(3-t)\left(9+3 t+t^{2}\right)} \\
> & =\lim _{t \rightarrow 3} \frac{\left(t^{2}-3\right)(t+3)}{-\left(9+3 t+t^{2}\right)}=\frac{(9-3)(3+3)}{-(9+9+9)}=\frac{-36}{27}=\frac{-4}{3} .
> \end{aligned}
