### Answer to Question 07

  

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-3/Chapter-20/Section-H/Q-07 

  

- Answer:

  

  

  

  

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Put $\sqrt{a^{2}-b^{2}}=c$. Then,
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \left(d_{1} \times d_{2}\right) & =\frac{\left|\frac{c \cos \theta}{a}+0-1\right|}{\sqrt{\frac{\cos ^{2} \theta}{a^{2}}+\frac{\sin ^{2} \theta}{b^{2}}}} \times \frac{\left|\frac{-c \cos \theta}{a}+0-1\right|}{\sqrt{\frac{\cos ^{2} \theta}{a^{2}}+\frac{\sin ^{2} \theta}{b^{2}}}}=\frac{\left|\frac{c \cos \theta}{a}-1\right|\left|\frac{c \cos \theta}{a}+1\right|}{\left(\frac{\cos ^{2} \theta}{a^{2}}+\frac{\sin ^{2} \theta}{b^{2}}\right)} \\
> & =\frac{\left|\frac{c^{2} \cos ^{2} \theta}{a^{2}}-1\right|}{\left(b^{2} \cos ^{2} \theta+a^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta\right)} \times a^{2} b^{2}=\frac{\left|\left(a^{2}-b^{2}\right) \cos ^{2} \theta-a^{2}\right|}{\left(b^{2} \cos ^{2} \theta+a^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta\right)} \times b^{2} \\
> & =\frac{\left|a^{2}\left(1-\sin ^{2} \theta\right)-b^{2} \cos ^{2} \theta-a^{2}\right|}{\left(b^{2} \cos ^{2}+a^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta\right)} \times b^{2}=\frac{\left|-\left(a^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta+b^{2} \cos ^{2} \theta\right)\right|}{\left(b^{2} \cos ^{2} \theta+a^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta\right)} \times b^{2} \\
> & =b^{2}
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
>
