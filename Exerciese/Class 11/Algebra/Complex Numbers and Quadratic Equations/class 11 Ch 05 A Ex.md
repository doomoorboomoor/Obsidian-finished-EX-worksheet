### Answer to Question 15

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-2/Chapter-05/Section-A/Q-15

- Answer:



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> $\sum_{n=1}^{13}\left(i^{n}+i^{n+1}\right)=\sum_{n=1}^{13} i^{n}(1+i)=(1+i) \cdot \sum_{n=1}^{13} i^{n}$
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & =(1+i) \cdot\left\{i+i^{2}+i^{3}+\ldots+i^{13}\right\} \\
> & =(1+i) \frac{a\left(1-r^{n}\right)}{(1-r)}, \text{ where } a=i, r=i \text{ and } n=13 \\
> & =(1+i) \cdot \frac{i\left(1-i^{13}\right)}{(1-i)}=i(1+i) \cdot \frac{(1-i)}{(1-i)}=(-1+i) \cdot\left[\because i^{13}=\left(i^{4}\right)^{3} \times i=1 \times i=i\right]
> \end{aligned}
> $$
