> as $B D=E D, \angle A D B=\angle A D E=90^{\circ}$ and $A D=A D$.
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \therefore \quad A B=A E & \Rightarrow \angle A B E=\angle A E D>\angle C \\
> & \Rightarrow \angle B>\angle C \Rightarrow A C>A B .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
