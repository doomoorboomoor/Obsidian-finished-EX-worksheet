### Answer to Question 20

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-09/Unit-4/Chapter-13/Section-A/Q-20

- Answer:







> [!INFO]
>
> Steps of Construction
>
> (i) Draw any line $X Y$.
>
> (ii) Take any point $D$ on $X Y$ and draw $D E \perp X Y$.
>
> (iii) Cut off $D A=4.5 \mathrm{~cm}$ along $D E$.
>
> (iv) Through $A$ draw $L M \parallel X Y$.
>
> (v) Construct $\angle L A B=45^{\circ}$ and $\angle M A C=60^{\circ}$, meeting $X Y$ at $B$ and $C$ respectively. Then, $\triangle A B C$ is the required triangle.
