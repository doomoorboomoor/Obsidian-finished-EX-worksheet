### Answer to Question 07/ii

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-09/Unit-4/Chapter-10/Section-A/Q-07/Q-ii

- Answer:



> [!INFO]
>
> From $\triangle C Q R, \angle R Q B=\angle Q C R+\angle Q R C$
> $\Rightarrow \angle R Q P+\angle P Q B=90^{\circ}+\angle Q R C$
