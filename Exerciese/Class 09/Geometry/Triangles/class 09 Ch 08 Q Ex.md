### Answer to Question 19

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-09/Unit-4/Chapter-08/Section-A/Q-19

- Answer:

$x=90$

> [!INFO]
>
> $\angle B A C=\left(180^{\circ}-108^{\circ}\right)=72^{\circ}$.
> 
> $\angle B A D: \angle C A D=1: 3$
> $\therefore \quad \angle B A D=\left(\frac{1}{4} \times 72^{\circ}\right)=18^{\circ}, \angle C A D=\left(\frac{3}{4} \times 72^{\circ}\right)=54^{\circ}$.
> $A D=D B \Rightarrow \angle D B A=\angle B A D=18^{\circ}$.
> $\angle A+\angle B+\angle C=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow 72^{\circ}+18^{\circ}+x^{\circ}=180^{\circ} \Rightarrow x=90$.

$\angle M A N=25^{\circ}$

> [!INFO]

> 
> In $\triangle A B M, \angle A B M+\angle B M A+\angle B A M=180^{\circ}$.
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \therefore \quad 70^{\circ}+90^{\circ}+\angle B A M=180^{\circ} & \Rightarrow \angle B A M=20^{\circ} . \\

> & \Rightarrow \angle M A N=\left(45^{\circ}-20^{\circ}\right)=25^{\circ} .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
