### Answer to Question 23.

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-09/Unit-2/Chapter-03/Section-G/Q-23

- Answer: 

108

> [!INFO]
>
> $(a+b+c)^{3}=[(a+b)+c]^{3}$
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & =(a+b)^{3}+c^{3}+3(a+b) c \times[(a+b)+c] \quad\left[\because(x+y)^{3}=x^{3}+y^{3}+3 x y(x+y)\right] \\
> & =a^{3}+b^{3}+c^{3}+3 a b(a+b)+3(a+b) \times\left[(a+b) c+c^{2}\right] \\
> & =a^{3}+b^{3}+c^{3}+3(a+b)\left[a b+a c+b c+c^{2}\right] \\
> & =a^{3}+b^{3}+c^{3}+3(a+b)[a(b+c)+c(b+c)] \\
> & =a^{3}+b^{3}+c^{3}+3(a+b)(b+c)(c+a)
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
> $\therefore(a+b+c)^{3}-a^{3}-b^{3}-c^{3}=3(a+b)(b+c)(c+a)$.
>
