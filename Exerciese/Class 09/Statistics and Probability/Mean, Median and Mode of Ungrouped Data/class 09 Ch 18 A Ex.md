### Answer to Question 26.

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-09/Unit-6/Chapter-18/Section-A/Q-26

- Answer: 

$12 \mathrm{~km} / \mathrm{hr}$

> [!INFO]
> 
> Let the distance of one side journey be $x \mathrm{~km}$. Then, total distance covered $=2 x \mathrm{~km}$.
> Total time taken $=\left(\frac{x}{15}+\frac{x}{10}\right) \mathrm{hr}=\left(\frac{5 x}{30}\right) \mathrm{hr}=\left(\frac{x}{6}\right) \mathrm{hr}$.
> Average speed for the whole journey $=\left\{\frac{2 x}{(x / 6)}\right\} \mathrm{km} / \mathrm{hr}=12 \mathrm{~km} / \mathrm{hr}$.
>
