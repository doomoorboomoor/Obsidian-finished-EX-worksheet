### Answer to Question 05

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-6/Chapter-17/Section-A/Q-05

- Answer:

$25: 64$

> [!INFO]
>
> Let the radii of the cones be $4 r$ and $5 r$ and their heights be $h_{1}$ and $h_{2}$ respectively. Then, $\frac{\frac{1}{3} \pi \times(4 r)^{2} \times h_{1}}{\frac{1}{3} \pi \times(5 r)^{2} \times h_{2}}=\frac{1}{4} \Rightarrow \frac{16 h_{1}}{25 h_{2}}=\frac{1}{4} \Rightarrow \frac{h_{1}}{h_{2}}=\frac{1}{4} \times \frac{25}{16}=\frac{25}{64}$.
