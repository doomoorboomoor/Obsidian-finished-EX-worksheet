### Answer to Question 06

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-6/Chapter-15/Section-A/Q-06

- Answer:

$60 \mathrm{~cm}^{2}$

> [!INFO]
>
> Sum of two sides $=(40-17) \mathrm{cm}=23 \mathrm{~cm}$.
> 
> Let these sides be $x \mathrm{~cm}$ and $(23-x) \mathrm{cm}$.
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \therefore \quad x^{2}+(23-x)^{2}=(17)^{2} & \Rightarrow x^{2}+x^{2}-46 x+529=289 \\
> & \Rightarrow 2 x^{2}-46 x+240=0 \Rightarrow x^{2}-23 x+120=0 \\
> & \Rightarrow(x-15)(x-8)=0 \Rightarrow x=15 \text{ or } x=8 .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
> $\therefore \quad$ base $=15 \mathrm{~cm}$ and height $=8 \mathrm{~cm}$. Find the area of the triangle.
