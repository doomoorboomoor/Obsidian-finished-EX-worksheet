### Answer to Question 06

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-4/Chapter-28/Section-B/Q-06

- Answer:

$p=3, \hat{n}=\left(\frac{-3}{13} \hat{i}+\frac{12}{13} \hat{j}+\frac{4}{13} \hat{k}\right)$



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Given equation in standard form is $\vec{r} \cdot(-3 \hat{i}+12 \hat{j}+4 \hat{k})=39$.
> 
> This is $\vec{r} \cdot \vec{n}=p$, where $\vec{n}=(-3 \hat{i}+12 \hat{j}+4 \hat{k})$ and $p=39$.
> 
> $$
> \begin{array}{ll} 
> & |\vec{n}|=\sqrt{(-3)^{2}+(12)^{2}+4^{2}}=\sqrt{9+144+16}=\sqrt{169}=13 . \\
> \therefore & \vec{r} \cdot \frac{\vec{n}}{|\vec{n}|}=\frac{p}{|\vec{n}|}=\frac{39}{13}=3 . \\
> & \vec{r} \cdot\left(\frac{-3}{13} \hat{i}+\frac{12}{13} \hat{j}+\frac{4}{13} \hat{k}\right)=3 .
> \end{array}
> $$
> 
> Hence, $p=3$ and $\hat{n}=\frac{-3}{13} \hat{i}+\frac{12}{13} \hat{j}+\frac{4}{13} \hat{k}$.
>

> 
> Given equation is $2 x+3 y+4 z=12$.
> 
> D.r.'s of normal to the plane are $2,3,4$.

> 
> $$

> $$
> 
> Hence, the required point is $\left(\frac{24}{29}, \frac{36}{29}, \frac{48}{29}\right)$.
