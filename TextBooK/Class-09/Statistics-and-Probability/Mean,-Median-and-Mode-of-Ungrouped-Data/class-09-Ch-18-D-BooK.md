## MODE OF UNGROUPED DATA

Mode is the most frequently occurring observation.

## SOLVED EXAMPLES

EXAMPLE 1 Find the mode of the following data:

$$
14,25,14,28,18,17,18,14,23,22,14,18 .
$$

solution Arranging the given data in an ascending order, we get

$$
14,14,14,14,17,18,18,18,22,23,25,28 .
$$

Here, 14 occurs most often.
Hence, mode of the given data $=14$.
EXAMPLE 2 The points scored by a basketball team in a series of matches are as follows:

$$
17,2,7,27,25,5,14,18,10,24,48,10,8,7,10,28 .
$$

Find the median and mode of the above data.
solution Arranging the scores in an ascending order, we get

$$
2,5,7,7,8,10,10,10,14,17,18,24,25,27,28,48 .
$$

Here, $n=16$ (even).

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text { Median score } & =\text { mean of }\left(\frac{n}{2}\right) \text { th and }\left(\frac{n}{2}+1\right) \text { th scores } \\
& =\text { mean of }\left(\frac{16}{2}\right) \text { th and }\left(\frac{16}{2}+1\right) \text { th scores } \\
& =\text { mean of } 8 \text { th and } 9 \text { th scores } \\
& =\left(\frac{10+14}{2}\right)=\frac{24}{2}=12
\end{aligned}
$$

Mode $=$ most occurring score $=10$.
EXAMPLE 3 The following number of goals were scored by a team in a series of 10 matches:

$$
2,3,4,5,0,1,3,3,4,3 .
$$

Find the mean, median and mode of these scores.
SOLUTION Arranging the number of goals scored in an ascending order, we get

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \qquad 0,1,2,3,3,3,3,4,4,5 \\
& \text { Mean }=\left(\frac{0+1+2+4 \times 3+2 \times 4+5}{10}\right)=\frac{28}{10}=2.8 \\
& \text { Mode }=\text { most occurring score }=3 \\
& \text { Total number of matches }=10(\text { even }) \\
& \begin{aligned}
\text { Median score } & =\text { mean of }\left(\frac{10}{2}\right) \text { th and }\left(\frac{10}{2}+1\right) \text { th scores } \\
& =\text { mean of } 5 \text { th and } 6 \text { th scores } \\
& =\left(\frac{3+3}{2}\right)=\frac{6}{2}=3
\end{aligned}
\end{aligned}
$$

$\therefore \quad$ mean $=2.8$, mode $=3$ and median $=3$.

EXAMPLE 4 In a mathematics test given to 15 students, the following marks (out of 100) are recorded:

$$
52,60,42,40,98,52,48,39,41,62,46,52,54,40,96 .
$$

Find the mean, median and mode of the given data.

SOLUTION

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text { Mean } & =\frac{\text { sum of the given observations }}{\text { total number of observations }} \\
& =\frac{822}{15}=\frac{274}{5}=54.8
\end{aligned}
$$

Now, we arrange the given data in an ascending order, as under:

$$
39,40,40,41,42,46,48,52,52,52,54,60,62,96,98 .
$$

In this data, the most occurring item is 52 .
$\therefore \quad$ mode $=52$.
Total number of terms $=15($ odd $)$.
$\therefore \quad$ median $=$ marks obtained by $\left(\frac{15+1}{2}\right)$ th student
$=$ marks obtained by 8th student $=52$.
$\therefore \quad$ mean $=54.8$, median $=52$ and mode $=52$.
