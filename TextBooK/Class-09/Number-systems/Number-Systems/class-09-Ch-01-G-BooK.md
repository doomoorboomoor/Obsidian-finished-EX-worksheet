## LAWS OF EXPONENTS

Let $a>0, b>0$ be real numbers and let $m$ and $n$ be rational numbers. Then, we have:
(i) $a^{m} \times a^{n}=a^{m+n}$
(ii) $\frac{a^{m}}{a^{n}}=a^{m-n}$
(iii) $\left(a^{m}\right)^{n}=a^{m n}$
(iv) $a^{m} \times b^{m}=(a b)^{m}$
(v) $(a b)^{m}=a^{m} b^{m}$
(vi) $\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^{m}=\frac{a^{m}}{b^{m}}$
(vii) $a^{-n}=\frac{1}{a^{n}}$
(viii) $a^{0}=1$

---

### Example 1: Simplify

(i) $3^{3 / 4} \times 3^{1 / 4}$
(ii) $\frac{5^{1 / 3}}{5^{1 / 6}}$
(iii) $3^{2 / 3} \times 7^{2 / 3}$

#### Solution:

Using the laws of exponents, we have:
(i) $3^{3 / 4} \times 3^{1 / 4}=3^{(3 / 4+1 / 4)}=3^{1}=3 \quad [\because a^{m} \times a^{n}=a^{(m+n)}]$
(ii) $\frac{5^{1 / 3}}{5^{1 / 6}}=5^{(1 / 3-1 / 6)}=5^{1 / 6} \quad [\because \frac{a^{m}}{a^{n}}=a^{(m-n)}]$
(iii) $3^{2 / 3} \times 7^{2 / 3}=(3 \times 7)^{2 / 3}=(21)^{2 / 3} \quad [\because a^{m} \times b^{m}=(a b)^{m}]$

---

### Example 2: Simplify

(i) $(36)^{1 / 2}$
(ii) $(64)^{1 / 3}$
(iii) $(32)^{1 / 5}$

#### Solution:

We have:
(i) $(36)^{1 / 2}=\left(6^{2}\right)^{1 / 2}=6^{\left(2 \times \frac{1}{2}\right)}=6^{1}=6$
(ii) $(64)^{1 / 3}=\left(4^{3}\right)^{1 / 3}=4^{\left(3 \times \frac{1}{3}\right)}=4^{1}=4$
(iii) $(32)^{1 / 5}=\left(2^{5}\right)^{1 / 5}=2^{\left(5 \times \frac{1}{5}\right)}=2^{1}=2$

---

### Example 3: Simplify

(i) $(16)^{3 / 2}$
(ii) $(27)^{2 / 3}$
(iii) $(125)^{-1 / 3}$

#### Solution:

We have:
(i) $(16)^{3 / 2}=\left(4^{2}\right)^{3 / 2}=4^{\left(2 \times \frac{3}{2}\right)}=4^{3}=64$
(ii) $(27)^{2 / 3}=\left(3^{3}\right)^{2 / 3}=3^{\left(3 \times \frac{2}{3}\right)}=3^{2}=9$
(iii) $(125)^{-1 / 3}=\frac{1}{(125)^{1 / 3}}=\frac{1}{\left(5^{3}\right)^{1 / 3}}=\frac{1}{5^{\left(3 \times \frac{1}{3}\right)}}=\frac{1}{5^{1}}=\frac{1}{5}$

## IMPORTANT RESULTS

**RESULT 1**
Let $a>0$ be a real number and $n$ be a positive integer. Then, the **n-th root of a** is defined as $\sqrt[n]{a}=b$, if $b^{n}=a$ and $b>0$.
In the language of exponents, we define $\sqrt[n]{a}=a^{1 / n}$.
Thus, we have $\sqrt[3]{2}=2^{\frac{1}{3}} ; \sqrt[4]{5}=5^{\frac{1}{4}}$, etc.

**RESULT 2**
Let $a>0$ be a real number and $\frac{m}{n}$ be a rational number, $n>0$. Then,
$$
a^{\frac{m}{n}}=\left(a^{m}\right)^{\frac{1}{n}}=\left(a^{1 / n}\right)^{m} \Rightarrow a^{\frac{m}{n}}=\sqrt[n]{a^{m}}=(\sqrt[n]{a})^{m}
$$
Thus, $4^{\frac{3}{2}}=\left(4^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}=(64)^{\frac{1}{2}}=\sqrt{64}=8$.
And, $4^{\frac{3}{2}}=\left(4^{1 / 2}\right)^{3}=(\sqrt{4})^{3}=2^{3}=8$.

(i) $(32)^{\frac{1}{5}}+(-7)^{0}+(64)^{\frac{1}{2}}$ [2010]

$$
(ii) $(0.00032)^{-\frac{2}{5}}=\frac{1}{(0.00032)^{\frac{2}{5}}} \quad [\because a^{-n}=\frac{1}{a^{n}}]$

\begin{aligned}
& =\frac{1}{\left[(0.2)^{5}\right]^{\frac{2}{5}}}=\frac{1}{(0.2)^{\left(5 \times \frac{2}{5}\right)}}=\frac{1}{(0.2)^{2}} \\
& =\frac{1}{0.04}=\frac{100}{4}=25
\end{aligned}
$$
(iii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(\frac{64}{125}\right)^{-\frac{2}{3}} & =\left(\frac{4^{3}}{5^{3}}\right)^{-\frac{2}{3}}=\left[\left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^{3}\right]^{-\frac{2}{3}} \\
& =\left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^{\left[3 \times\left(-\frac{2}{3}\right)\right]}=\left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^{-2}=\left(\frac{5}{4}\right)^{2}=\frac{25}{16}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 3: Assuming that $x, y, z$ are positive real numbers, simplify each of the following.

(i) $\sqrt{x^{3} y^{-2}}$
(ii) $\left(x^{-2 / 3} y^{-1 / 2}\right)^{2}$
(iii) $\left(\frac{x^{-4}}{y^{-10}}\right)^{\frac{5}{4}}$
(iv) $\sqrt[4]{81 x^{8} y^{4} z^{16}} \div \sqrt[3]{27 x^{3} y^{6} z^{9}}$
(v) $\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{3} \sqrt[3]{x^{2} \sqrt{x}}}}$
(vi) $(\sqrt{x})^{-\frac{2}{3}} \sqrt{y^{4}} \div \sqrt{x y^{-\frac{1}{2}}} \sqrt{x^{-2} y^{3}}$

#### Solution:

(i) $\sqrt{x^{3} y^{-2}}=\sqrt{\frac{x^{3}}{y^{2}}}=\left(\frac{x^{3}}{y^{2}}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}=\frac{\left(x^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}}{\left(y^{2}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}}=\frac{x^{\left(3 \times \frac{1}{2}\right)}}{y^{\left(2 \times \frac{1}{2}\right)}}=\frac{x^{\frac{3}{2}}}{y}$
(ii)
$$
\left(x^{-\frac{2}{3}} y^{-\frac{1}{2}}\right)^{2}=\left(x^{-\frac{2}{3}}\right)^{2}\left(y^{-\frac{1}{2}}\right)^{2}=x^{\left[\left(-\frac{2}{3}\right) \times 2\right]} \cdot y^{\left[\left(-\frac{1}{2}\right) \times 2\right]}
$$
$$
=x^{-\frac{4}{3}} \cdot y^{-1}=\frac{1}{x^{\frac{4}{3}} y}
$$
(iii) $\left(\frac{x^{-4}}{y^{-10}}\right)^{\frac{5}{4}}=\left(\frac{y^{10}}{x^{4}}\right)^{\frac{5}{4}}=\frac{\left(y^{10}\right)^{\frac{5}{4}}}{\left(x^{4}\right)^{\frac{5}{4}}}=\frac{y^{\left(10 \times \frac{5}{4}\right)}}{x^{\left(4 \times \frac{5}{4}\right)}}=\frac{y^{\frac{25}{2}}}{x^{5}}$
(iv) $\sqrt[4]{81 x^{8} y^{4} z^{16}} \div \sqrt[3]{27 x^{3} y^{6} z^{9}}$
$$
\begin{aligned}
& =\frac{\left(81 x^{8} y^{4} z^{16}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}}}{\left(27 x^{3} y^{6} z^{9}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}}}=\frac{(81)^{\frac{1}{4}}\left(x^{8}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} \cdot\left(y^{4}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} \cdot\left(z^{16}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}}}{(27)^{\frac{1}{3}} \cdot\left(x^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}} \cdot\left(y^{6}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}} \cdot\left(z^{9}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}}} \\
& =\frac{\left(3^{4}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}} \cdot x^{\left(8 \times \frac{1}{4}\right)} \cdot y^{\left(4 \times \frac{1}{4}\right)} \cdot z^{\left(16 \times \frac{1}{4}\right)}}{\left(3^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}} \cdot x^{\left(3 \times \frac{1}{3}\right)} \cdot y^{\left(6 \times \frac{1}{3}\right)} \cdot z^{\left(9 \times \frac{1}{3}\right)}}=\frac{3^{\left(4 \times \frac{1}{4}\right)} \cdot x^{2} \cdot y z^{4}}{3^{\left(3 \times \frac{1}{3}\right)} \cdot x y^{2} z^{3}} \\
& =\frac{x^{(2-1)} \cdot z^{(4-3)}}{y^{(2-1)}}=\frac{x z}{y}
\end{aligned}
$$
(v)
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{3} \sqrt[3]{x^{2} \sqrt{x}}}} &=\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{3} \sqrt[3]{x^{2} \cdot x^{1 / 2}}}}=\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{3} \sqrt[3]{x^{\left(2+\frac{1}{2}\right)}}}} \\
& =\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{3} \sqrt[3]{x^{5 / 2}}}}=\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{3} \cdot\left(x^{5 / 2}\right)^{1 / 3}}} \\
& =\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{3} \cdot x^{5 / 6}}}=\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{\left(3+\frac{5}{6}\right)}}}=\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \sqrt[4]{x^{\left(\frac{23}{6}\right)}}} \\
& =\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \cdot\left(x^{\frac{23}{6}}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}}}=\sqrt[5]{x^{4} \cdot x^{\frac{23}{24}}}=\sqrt[5]{x^{\left(4+\frac{23}{24}\right)}}=\left(x^{\frac{119}{24}}\right)^{\frac{1}{5}} \\
& =x^{\left(\frac{119}{24} \times \frac{1}{5}\right)}=x^{\frac{119}{120}}
\end{aligned}
$$
(vi)
$$
\begin{aligned}
(\sqrt{x})^{-\frac{2}{3}} \sqrt{y^{4}} \div \sqrt{x y^{-\frac{1}{2}}} \sqrt{x^{-2} y^{3}} & =\frac{\left(x^{\frac{1}{2}}\right)^{-\frac{2}{3}} \cdot\left(y^{4}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}}{\left(x y^{-\frac{1}{2}}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}\left(x^{-2} y^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}}=\frac{x^{\left[\left(\frac{1}{2}\right) \times\left(-\frac{2}{3}\right)\right]} y^{\left(4 \times \frac{1}{2}\right)}}{x^{\frac{1}{2}} \cdot\left(y^{-\frac{1}{2}}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}} \cdot\left(x^{-2}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}} \cdot\left(y^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}}} \\
& =\frac{x^{-\frac{1}{3}} \cdot y^{2}}{x^{\frac{1}{2}} \cdot y^{\left[\left(-\frac{1}{2}\right) \times \frac{1}{2}\right]} \cdot x^{\left[(-2) \times \frac{1}{2}\right]} \cdot y^{\left(3 \times \frac{1}{2}\right)}} \\
& =\frac{x^{-\frac{1}{3}} \cdot y^{2}}{x^{\frac{1}{2}} \cdot y^{-\frac{1}{4}} \cdot x^{-1} \cdot y^{\frac{3}{2}}}=x^{\left(-\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{2}+1\right)} \cdot y^{\left(2+\frac{1}{4}-\frac{3}{2}\right)} \\
& =x^{\frac{1}{6}} \cdot y^{\frac{3}{4}}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 4: Simplify

$$
\left(\frac{81}{16}\right)^{-\frac{3}{4}} \times\left[\left(\frac{25}{9}\right)^{-\frac{3}{2}} \div\left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^{-3}\right]
$$

#### Solution:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(\frac{81}{16}\right)^{-\frac{3}{4}} \times\left[\left(\frac{25}{9}\right)^{-\frac{3}{2}} \div\left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^{-3}\right] &=\left(\frac{3^{4}}{2^{4}}\right)^{-\frac{3}{4}} \times\left[\left(\frac{5^{2}}{3^{2}}\right)^{-\frac{3}{2}} \div\left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^{-3}\right] \\
& =\left[\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{4}\right]^{-\frac{3}{4}} \times\left[\left\{\left(\frac{5}{3}\right)^{2}\right\}^{-\frac{3}{2}} \div\left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^{-3}\right]=\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{4 \times\left(-\frac{3}{4}\right)} \times\left[\left(\frac{5}{3}\right)^{2 \times\left(-\frac{3}{2}\right)} \div\left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^{-3}\right] \\
& =\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{-3} \times\left[\left(\frac{5}{3}\right)^{-3} \div\left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^{-3}\right]=\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{3} \times\left[\left(\frac{3}{5}\right)^{3} \div\left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^{3}\right] \\
& =\frac{2^{3}}{3^{3}} \times\left[\frac{3^{3}}{5^{3}} \div \frac{2^{3}}{5^{3}}\right]=\frac{2^{3}}{3^{3}} \times\left[\frac{3^{3}}{5^{3}} \times \frac{5^{3}}{2^{3}}\right]=\frac{2^{3}}{3^{3}} \times \frac{3^{3}}{2^{3}}=1
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 5: Prove that $\frac{2^{x-1}+2^{x}}{2^{x+1}-2^{x}}=\frac{3}{2}$.

#### Solution:

We have
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{2^{x-1}+2^{x}}{2^{x+1}-2^{x}} & =\frac{2^{x-1}(1+2)}{2^{x}(2-1)}=\frac{3 \cdot 2^{x-1}}{2^{x}}=3 \cdot 2^{x-1-x} \\
& =3 \cdot 2^{-1}=\frac{3}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 6: Prove that $\frac{2^{30}+2^{29}+2^{28}}{2^{31}+2^{30}-2^{29}}=\frac{7}{10}$.

#### Solution:

We have
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{2^{30}+2^{29}+2^{28}}{2^{31}+2^{30}-2^{29}} & =\frac{2^{28}\left(2^{2}+2+1\right)}{2^{29}\left(2^{2}+2-1\right)}=\frac{2^{28}(4+2+1)}{2^{29}(4+2-1)} \\
& =\frac{7}{5} \times \frac{1}{2^{(29-28)}}=\frac{7}{5 \times 2}=\frac{7}{10}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 7: Prove that

(i) $\frac{a^{-1}}{a^{-1}+b^{-1}}+\frac{a^{-1}}{a^{-1}-b^{-1}}=\frac{2 b^{2}}{b^{2}-a^{2}}$
(ii) $\frac{1}{1+x^{a-b}}+\frac{1}{1+x^{b-a}}=1$

#### Solution:

We have
(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{a^{-1}}{a^{-1}+b^{-1}}+\frac{a^{-1}}{a^{-1}-b^{-1}} & =\frac{\frac{1}{a}}{\frac{1}{a}+\frac{1}{b}}+\frac{\frac{1}{a}}{\frac{1}{a}-\frac{1}{b}}=\frac{\frac{1}{a}}{\left(\frac{b+a}{a b}\right)}+\frac{\frac{1}{a}}{\left(\frac{b-a}{a b}\right)} \\
& =\frac{1}{a} \cdot \frac{a b}{(b+a)}+\frac{1}{a} \cdot \frac{a b}{(b-a)}=\frac{b}{b+a}+\frac{b}{b-a} \\
& =\frac{b(b-a)+b(b+a)}{(b+a)(b-a)}=\frac{b^{2}-a b+b^{2}+a b}{b^{2}-a^{2}} \\
& =\frac{2 b^{2}}{b^{2}-a^{2}}
\end{aligned}
$$
(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{1}{1+x^{a-b}}+\frac{1}{1+x^{b-a}} & =\frac{1}{1+x^{a} \cdot x^{-b}}+\frac{1}{1+x^{b} \cdot x^{-a}} \\
& =\frac{1}{1+\frac{x^{a}}{x^{b}}}+\frac{1}{1+\frac{x^{b}}{x^{a}}}=\frac{1}{\left(\frac{x^{a}+x^{b}}{x^{b}}\right)}+\frac{1}{\left(\frac{x^{a}+x^{b}}{x^{a}}\right)} \\
& =\frac{x^{b}}{x^{a}+x^{b}}+\frac{x^{a}}{x^{a}+x^{b}}=\frac{x^{b}+x^{a}}{x^{a}+x^{b}}=1
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 8: Simplify

$$
\begin{align*}
& (i) \left(\frac{x^{l}}{x^{m}}\right)^{\frac{1}{l m}} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{m}}{x^{n}}\right)^{\frac{1}{m n}} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{n}}{x^{l}}\right)^{\frac{1}{n l}} \tag{2011} \\
& (ii) \left(\frac{x^{p}}{x^{q}}\right)^{p+q} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{q}}{x^{r}}\right)^{q+r} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{r}}{x^{p}}\right)^{r+p} \tag{2011}
\end{align*}
$$

#### Solution:

We have
(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(\frac{x^{l}}{x^{m}}\right)^{\frac{1}{l m}} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{m}}{x^{n}}\right)^{\frac{1}{m n}} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{n}}{x^{l}}\right)^{\frac{1}{n l}} & \quad [\because \frac{a^{p}}{a^{q}}=a^{p-q}] \\
& =\left(x^{l-m}\right)^{\frac{1}{l m}} \cdot\left(x^{m-n}\right)^{\frac{1}{m n}} \cdot\left(x^{n-l}\right)^{\frac{1}{n l}} \\
& =x^{\frac{l-m}{l m}} \cdot x^{\frac{m-n}{m n}} \cdot x^{\frac{n-l}{n l}} \\
& =x^{\frac{l-m}{l m}+\frac{m-n}{m n}+\frac{n-l}{n l}} \\
& =x^{\frac{n(l-m)+l(m-n)+m(n-l)}{l m n}}=x^{\frac{n l-n m+l m-l n+m n-m l}{l m n}} \\
& =x^{0}=1
\end{aligned}
$$
(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(\frac{x^{p}}{x^{q}}\right)^{p+q} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{q}}{x^{r}}\right)^{q+r} \cdot\left(\frac{x^{r}}{x^{p}}\right)^{r+p} & =\left(x^{p-q}\right)^{p+q} \cdot\left(x^{q-r}\right)^{q+r} \cdot\left(x^{r-p}\right)^{r+p} \\
& =x^{(p-q)(p+q)} \cdot x^{(q-r)(q+r)} \cdot x^{(r-p)(r+p)} \\
& =x^{\left(p^{2}-q^{2}\right)} \cdot x^{\left(q^{2}-r^{2}\right)} \cdot x^{\left(r^{2}-p^{2}\right)} \\
& =x^{\left(p^{2}-q^{2}\right)+\left(q^{2}-r^{2}\right)+\left(r^{2}-p^{2}\right)}=x^{0}=1
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 9:

(i) If $x^{\frac{1}{12}}=49^{\frac{1}{24}}$, find the value of $x$.
(ii) If $(125)^{x}=\frac{25}{5^{x}}$, find $x$.

#### Solution:

We have
(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
x^{\frac{1}{12}}=49^{\frac{1}{24}} & \Leftrightarrow\left(x^{\frac{1}{12}}\right)^{12}=\left(49^{\frac{1}{24}}\right)^{12} \\
& \Leftrightarrow x^{\left(\frac{1}{12} \times 12\right)}=49^{\left(\frac{1}{24} \times 12\right)} \\
& \Leftrightarrow x=49^{\frac{1}{2}}=\sqrt{49}=7
\end{aligned}
$$
(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
(125)^{x}=\frac{25}{5^{x}} & \Leftrightarrow\left(5^{3}\right)^{x}=\frac{5^{2}}{5^{x}} \\
& \Leftrightarrow 5^{3 x}=5^{2-x} \\
& \Leftrightarrow 3 x=2-x \Rightarrow 4 x=2 \Rightarrow x=\frac{2}{4}=\frac{1}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 11: Write the following in the descending order of magnitude. [2010]

$$
\sqrt[3]{2}, \sqrt{3}, \sqrt[6]{5}
$$

#### Solution:

We have $\sqrt[3]{2}=2^{\frac{1}{3}} ; \sqrt{3}=3^{\frac{1}{2}} ; \sqrt[6]{5}=5^{\frac{1}{6}}$.
The LCM of 3, 2 and 6 is 6.
$$
\begin{aligned}
\therefore \quad 2^{\frac{1}{3}} & =2^{\left(\frac{1}{3} \times \frac{2}{2}\right)}=2^{\frac{2}{6}}=\left(2^{2}\right)^{\frac{1}{6}}=4^{\frac{1}{6}} \\
3^{\frac{1}{2}} & =3^{\left(\frac{1}{2} \times \frac{3}{3}\right)}=3^{\frac{3}{6}}=\left(3^{3}\right)^{\frac{1}{6}}=(27)^{\frac{1}{6}}
\end{aligned}
$$
Clearly, $(27)^{\frac{1}{6}}>5^{\frac{1}{6}}>4^{\frac{1}{6}}$.
So, $3^{\frac{1}{2}}>5^{\frac{1}{6}}>2^{\frac{1}{3}}$ or $\sqrt{3}>\sqrt[6]{5}>\sqrt[3]{2}$.
Hence, the correct descending order is $\sqrt{3}, \sqrt[6]{5}$ and $\sqrt[3]{2}$.
