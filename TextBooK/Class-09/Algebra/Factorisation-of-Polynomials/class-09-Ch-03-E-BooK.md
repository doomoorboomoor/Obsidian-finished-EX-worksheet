## Cube of a Binomial

**Formulae:**
1.  $(x+y)^{3}=x^{3}+y^{3}+3 x y(x+y)$
2.  $(x-y)^{3}=x^{3}-y^{3}-3 x y(x-y)$

---

## Solved Examples

### Example 1

Expand (i) $(4 a+5 b)^{3} \quad$ (ii) $(3 a-2 b)^{3}$

#### Solution:

(i) Putting $4 a=x$ and $5 b=y$, we get

$$
\begin{aligned}
(4 a+5 b)^{3} & =(x+y)^{3} \\
& =x^{3}+y^{3}+3 x y(x+y) \\
& =(4 a)^{3}+(5 b)^{3}+(3 \times 4 a \times 5 b)(4 a+5 b) \\
& =64 a^{3}+125 b^{3}+60 a b(4 a+5 b) \\
& =64 a^{3}+125 b^{3}+240 a^{2} b+300 a b^{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) Putting $3 a=x$ and $2 b=y$, we get

$$
\begin{aligned}
(3 a-2 b)^{3} & =(x-y)^{3} \\
& =x^{3}-y^{3}-3 x y(x-y) \\
& =(3 a)^{3}-(2 b)^{3}-(3 \times 3 a \times 2 b)(3 a-2 b) \\
& =27 a^{3}-8 b^{3}-18 a b(3 a-2 b) \\
& =27 a^{3}-8 b^{3}-54 a^{2} b+36 a b^{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 2

Expand (i) $\left(\frac{1}{x}+\frac{y}{3}\right)^{3} \quad$ (ii) $\left(4-\frac{1}{3 x}\right)^{3}$

#### Solution:

(i) Putting $\frac{1}{x}=a$ and $\frac{y}{3}=b$, we get

$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(\frac{1}{x}+\frac{y}{3}\right)^{3} & =(a+b)^{3} \\
& =a^{3}+b^{3}+3 a b(a+b) \\
& =\left(\frac{1}{x}\right)^{3}+\left(\frac{y}{3}\right)^{3}+3 \times \frac{1}{x} \times \frac{y}{3} \times\left(\frac{1}{x}+\frac{y}{3}\right) \\
& =\frac{1}{x^{3}}+\frac{y^{3}}{27}+\frac{y}{x}\left(\frac{1}{x}+\frac{y}{3}\right) \\
& =\frac{1}{x^{3}}+\frac{y^{3}}{27}+\frac{y}{x^{2}}+\frac{y^{2}}{3 x}=\frac{1}{x^{3}}+\frac{y}{x^{2}}+\frac{y^{2}}{3 x}+\frac{y^{3}}{27} .
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) Putting $4=a$ and $\frac{1}{3 x}=b$, we get

$$
\left(4-\frac{1}{3 x}\right)^{3}=(a-b)^{3}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& =a^{3}-b^{3}-3 a b(a-b) \\
& =(4)^{3}-\left(\frac{1}{3 x}\right)^{3}-3 \times 4 \times \frac{1}{3 x} \times\left(4-\frac{1}{3 x}\right) \\
& =64-\frac{1}{27 x^{3}}-\frac{16}{x}+\frac{4}{3 x^{2}}=64-\frac{16}{x}+\frac{4}{3 x^{2}}-\frac{1}{27 x^{3}}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 3

Simplify $(2 x-5 y)^{3}-(2 x+5 y)^{3}$.

#### Solution:

Putting $2 x=a$ and $5 y=b$, we get

$$
\begin{aligned}
& (2 x-5 y)^{3}-(2 x+5 y)^{3} \\
= & (a-b)^{3}-(a+b)^{3} \\
= & \left\{a^{3}-b^{3}-3 a b(a-b)\right\}-\left\{a^{3}+b^{3}+3 a b(a+b)\right\} \\
= & \left(a^{3}-b^{3}-3 a^{2} b+3 a b^{2}-a^{3}-b^{3}-3 a^{2} b-3 a b^{2}\right) \\
= & -2 b^{3}-6 a^{2} b=-2 \times(5 y)^{3}-6 \times(2 x)^{2} \times(5 y) \\
= & \left(-2 \times 125 y^{3}\right)-\left(6 \times 4 x^{2} \times 5 y\right)=-250 y^{3}-120 x^{2} y .
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 4

Factorise
(i) $8 a^{3}+b^{3}+12 a^{2} b+6 a b^{2}$
(ii) $64 a^{3}-27 b^{3}-144 a^{2} b+108 a b^{2}$

#### Solution:

We have

(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
& 8 a^{3}+b^{3}+12 a^{2} b+6 a b^{2} \\
= & (2 a)^{3}+b^{3}+6 a b(2 a+b) \\
= & (2 a)^{3}+b^{3}+(3 \times 2 a \times b)(2 a+b) \\
= & (2 a+b)^{3}=(2 a+b)(2 a+b)(2 a+b) .
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
& 64 a^{3}-27 b^{3}-144 a^{2} b+108 a b^{2} \\
= & (4 a)^{3}-(3 b)^{3}-36 a b(4 a-3 b) \\
= & (4 a)^{3}-(3 b)^{3}-(3 \times 4 a \times 3 b)(4 a-3 b) \\
= & (4 a-3 b)^{3}=(4 a-3 b)(4 a-3 b)(4 a-3 b) .
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 5

Factorise
(i) $27-125 a^{3}-135 a+225 a^{2}$
(ii) $1-64 a^{3}-12 a+48 a^{2}$

#### Solution:

We have

(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
& 27-125 a^{3}-135 a+225 a^{2} \\
= & 3^{3}-(5 a)^{3}-45 a(3-5 a) \\
= & 3^{3}-(5 a)^{3}-(3 \times 3 \times 5 a)(3-5 a) \\
= & (3-5 a)^{3}=(3-5 a)(3-5 a)(3-5 a)
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
& 1-64 a^{3}-12 a+48 a^{2} \\
= & 1^{3}-(4 a)^{3}-12 a(1-4 a) \\
= & 1^{3}-(4 a)^{3}-(3 \times 1 \times 4 a)(1-4 a) \\
= & (1-4 a)^{3}=(1-4 a)(1-4 a)(1-4 a)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 6

Factorise
(i) $8 p^{3}+\frac{12}{5} p^{2}+\frac{6}{25} p+\frac{1}{125}$
(ii) $27 p^{3}-\frac{9}{2} p^{2}+\frac{1}{4} p-\frac{1}{216}$

#### Solution:

We have

(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
& 8 p^{3}+\frac{12}{5} p^{2}+\frac{6}{25} p+\frac{1}{125} \\
= & 8 p^{3}+\frac{1}{125}+\frac{12}{5} p^{2}+\frac{6}{25} p \\
= & (2 p)^{3}+\left(\frac{1}{5}\right)^{3}+\frac{6}{5} p\left(2 p+\frac{1}{5}\right) \\
= & (2 p)^{3}+\left(\frac{1}{5}\right)^{3}+\left(3 \times 2 p \times \frac{1}{5}\right) \times\left(2 p+\frac{1}{5}\right) \\
= & \left(2 p+\frac{1}{5}\right)^{3}=\left(2 p+\frac{1}{5}\right)\left(2 p+\frac{1}{5}\right)\left(2 p+\frac{1}{5}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
& 27 p^{3}-\frac{9}{2} p^{2}+\frac{1}{4} p-\frac{1}{216} \\
= & 27 p^{3}-\frac{1}{216}-\frac{9}{2} p^{2}+\frac{1}{4} p \\
= & (3 p)^{3}-\left(\frac{1}{6}\right)^{3}-\frac{3}{2} p\left(3 p-\frac{1}{6}\right) \\
= & (3 p)^{3}-\left(\frac{1}{6}\right)^{3}-\left(3 \times 3 p \times \frac{1}{6}\right)\left(3 p-\frac{1}{6}\right) \\
= & \left(3 p-\frac{1}{6}\right)^{3}=\left(3 p-\frac{1}{6}\right)\left(3 p-\frac{1}{6}\right)\left(3 p-\frac{1}{6}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 7

Simplify $(x+y)^{3}-(x-y)^{3}-6 y\left(x^{2}-y^{2}\right)$.

#### Solution:

We have

$$
\begin{aligned}
& (x+y)^{3}-(x-y)^{3}-6 y\left(x^{2}-y^{2}\right) \\
= & (x+y)^{3}-(x-y)^{3}-3(x+y)(x-y) \times\{(x+y)-(x-y)\} \\
& {[\because 2 y=(x+y)-(x-y)] } \\
= & a^{3}-b^{3}-3 a b(a-b), \text { where } x+y=a \text { and } x-y=b \\
= & (a-b)^{3}=\{(x+y)-(x-y)\}^{3}=(2 y)^{3}=8 y^{3} .
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 8

Evaluate (i) $(102)^{3} \quad$ (ii) $(999)^{3}$

#### Solution:

We have

(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
(102)^{3} & =(100+2)^{3} \\
& =(100)^{3}+2^{3}+3 \times 100 \times 2 \times(100+2) \\
& =1000000+8+(600 \times 102) \\
& =1000008+61200=1061208
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
(999)^{3} & =(1000-1)^{3} \\
& =(1000)^{3}-1^{3}-(3 \times 1000 \times 1)(1000-1) \\
& =1000000000-1-(3000 \times 999) \\
& =999999999-2997000=997002999 .
\end{aligned}
$$
