## Square of a Trinomial

**Formula:**
$$(x+y+z)^{2}=x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}+2 x y+2 y z+2 z x$$

---

## Solved Examples

### Example 1:
**Expand** $(2 a+3 b+4 c)^{2}$.

#### Solution:
Putting $2a=x$, $3b=y$, and $4c=z$, we get:
$$
\begin{aligned}
& (2 a+3 b+4 c)^{2} \\
= & (x+y+z)^{2} \\
= & x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}+2 x y+2 y z+2 z x \\
= & (2 a)^{2}+(3 b)^{2}+(4 c)^{2}+\{2 \times(2 a) \times(3 b)\}+\{2 \times(3 b) \times(4 c)\} \\
& +\{2 \times(4 c) \times(2 a)\} \\
= & 4 a^{2}+9 b^{2}+16 c^{2}+12 a b+24 b c+16 c a .
\end{aligned}
$$

---

### Example 2:
**Expand** each of the following:
1.  $(4 a-b+2 c)^{2}$
2.  $(3 a-5 b-c)^{2}$
3.  $(-x+2 y-3 z)^{2}$

#### Solution:
**(i)** Putting $4a=x$, $-b=y$, and $2c=z$, we get:
$$
\begin{aligned}
& (4 a-b+2 c)^{2} \\
= & (x+y+z)^{2} \\
= & x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}+2 x y+2 y z+2 z x \\
= & (4 a)^{2}+(-b)^{2}+(2 c)^{2}+\{2 \times 4 a \times(-b)\}+\{2 \times(-b) \times 2 c\} \\
& +\{2 \times 2 c \times 4 a\} \\
= & 16 a^{2}+b^{2}+4 c^{2}-8 a b-4 b c+16 c a .
\end{aligned}
$$

**(ii)** Putting $3a=x$, $-5b=y$, and $-c=z$, we get:
$$
\begin{aligned}
& (3 a-5 b-c)^{2} \\
= & (x+y+z)^{2} \\
= & x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}+2 x y+2 y z+2 z x \\
= & (3 a)^{2}+(-5 b)^{2}+(-c)^{2}+\{2 \times 3 a \times(-5 b)\}+\{2 \times(-5 b) \times(-c)\} \\
& +\{2 \times(-c) \times 3 a\} \\
= & 9 a^{2}+25 b^{2}+c^{2}-30 a b+10 b c-6 c a .
\end{aligned}
$$

**(iii)** Putting $-x=a$, $2y=b$, and $-3z=c$, we get:
$$
\begin{aligned}
& (-x+2 y-3 z)^{2} \\
= & (a+b+c)^{2} \\
= & a^{2}+b^{2}+c^{2}+2 a b+2 b c+2 c a \\
= & (-x)^{2}+(2 y)^{2}+(-3 z)^{2}+\{2 \times(-x) \times 2 y\}+\{2 \times(2 y) \times(-3 z)\} \\
& +\{2 \times(-3 z) \times(-x)\} \\
= & x^{2}+4 y^{2}+9 z^{2}-4 x y-12 y z+6 z x .
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 3:
**Factorise** $9 x^{2}+4 y^{2}+16 z^{2}+12 x y-16 y z-24 x z$.

#### Solution:
We observe here that $-16yz$ and $-24xz$ are negative terms in the given expression and $z$ occurs in both the terms. So, we write $16z^{2}$ as $(-4z)^{2}$.

Thus, we have:
$$
\begin{aligned}
& 9 x^{2}+4 y^{2}+16 z^{2}+12 x y-16 y z-24 x z \\
= & (3 x)^{2}+(2 y)^{2}+(-4 z)^{2}+\{(2 \times(3 x) \times(2 y)\}+ \\
& +\{2 \times(2 y) \times(-4 z)\} \\
& +\{2 \times(-4 z) \times(3 x)\} \\
= & \{3 x+2 y+(-4 z)\}^{2}=(3 x+2 y-4 z)^{2} .
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 4:
**Factorise** $25 x^{2}+16 y^{2}+4 z^{2}-40 x y+16 y z-20 x z$.

#### Solution:
We observe here that $-40xy$ and $-20xz$ are negative terms in the given expression and $x$ occurs in both the terms. So, we write, $25x^{2}$ as $(-5x)^{2}$.
$$
\begin{aligned}
\therefore \quad & 25 x^{2}+16 y^{2}+4 z^{2}-40 x y+16 y z-20 x z \\
= & (-5 x)^{2}+(4 y)^{2}+(2 z)^{2}+\{2 \times(-5 x) \times(4 y)\}+\{2 \times(4 y) \times(2 z)\} \\
& +\{2 \times(-5 x) \times(2 z)\} \\
= & \{(-5 x)+4 y+2 z)\}^{2}=(-5 x+4 y+2 z)^{2} .
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 5:
**Factorise** $2 x^{2}+y^{2}+8 z^{2}-2 \sqrt{2} x y+4 \sqrt{2} y z-8 z x$.

#### Solution:
We observe here that $-2\sqrt{2}xy$ and $-8zx$ are negative terms in the given expression and $x$ occurs in both the terms. So, we write $2x^{2}$ as $(-\sqrt{2}x)^{2}$.
$$
\begin{aligned}
\therefore \quad & 2 x^{2}+y^{2}+8 z^{2}-2 \sqrt{2} x y+4 \sqrt{2} y z-8 z x \\
= & (-\sqrt{2} x)^{2}+y^{2}+(2 \sqrt{2} z)^{2}+\{2 \times(-\sqrt{2} x) \times y\} \\
& +\{2 \times y \times(2 \sqrt{2} z)\}+\{2 \times(2 \sqrt{2} z) \times(-\sqrt{2} x)\} \\
= & (-\sqrt{2} x+y+2 \sqrt{2} z)^{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 6:
**Evaluate**
(i) $101 \times 102$
(ii) $(999)^{2}$
(iii) $(997)^{2}$

#### Solution:
We have:

**(i)**
$$
\begin{aligned}
101 \times 102 & =101 \times(100+2) \\
& =(101 \times 100)+(101 \times 2)=10100+202=10302 .
\end{aligned}
$$

**(ii)**
$$
\begin{aligned}
(999)^{2} & =(1000-1)^{2} \\
& =(1000)^{2}+1^{2}-(2 \times 1000 \times 1) \quad [\because (a-b)^{2}=a^{2}+b^{2}-2 a b] \\
& =1000000+1-2000 \\
& =(1000001-2000)=998001 .
\end{aligned}
$$

**(iii)**
$$
\begin{aligned}
(997)^{2} & =(1000-3)^{2} \\
& =(1000)^{2}+3^{2}-(2 \times 1000 \times 3) \\
& =1000000+9-6000=994009 .
\end{aligned}
$$
