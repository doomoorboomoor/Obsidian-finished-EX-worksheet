## Factorisation of Sum or Difference of Cubes

**Theorem:** Prove that
1.  $(x^{3}+y^{3})=(x+y)(x^{2}-xy+y^{2})$
2.  $(x^{3}-y^{3})=(x-y)(x^{2}+xy+y^{2})$

---

### Proof

We have:

(i) We know that $(x+y)^{3}=(x^{3}+y^{3})+3xy(x+y)$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
\Rightarrow \quad (x^{3}+y^{3}) &= (x+y)^{3}-3xy(x+y) \\
&= (x+y) \times [(x+y)^{2}-3xy] \\
&= (x+y)(x^{2}+2xy+y^{2}-3xy) \\
&= (x+y)(x^{2}-xy+y^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\therefore (x^{3}+y^{3}) = (x+y)(x^{2}-xy+y^{2})
$$

(ii) We know that $(x-y)^{3}=(x^{3}-y^{3})-3xy(x-y)$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
\Rightarrow \quad (x^{3}-y^{3}) &= (x-y)^{3}+3xy(x-y) \\
&= (x-y) \times [(x-y)^{2}+3xy] \\
&= (x-y)(x^{2}-2xy+y^{2}+3xy) \\
&= (x-y)(x^{2}+xy+y^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\therefore (x^{3}-y^{3}) = (x-y)(x^{2}+xy+y^{2})
$$

---

## Summary

1.  $(x^{3}+y^{3})=(x+y)(x^{2}-xy+y^{2})$
2.  $(x^{3}-y^{3})=(x-y)(x^{2}+xy+y^{2})$

## Solved Examples

### Example: 1

Find the following products:
(i) $(\frac{x}{2}+2y)(\frac{x^{2}}{4}-xy+4y^{2})$
(ii) $(x^{2}-1)(x^{4}+x^{2}+1)$

#### Solution:

We have:

(i) $(\frac{x}{2}+2y)(\frac{x^{2}}{4}-xy+4y^{2})$
Let $a = \frac{x}{2}$ and $b = 2y$. The expression is in the form $(a+b)(a^{2}-ab+b^{2})$, which equals $(a^{3}+b^{3})$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= \left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^{3}+(2y)^{3} \\
&= \left(\frac{x^{3}}{8}+8y^{3}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) $(x^{2}-1)(x^{4}+x^{2}+1)$
Let $a = x^{2}$ and $b = 1$. The expression is in the form $(a-b)(a^{2}+ab+b^{2})$, which equals $(a^{3}-b^{3})$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (x^{2})^{3}-(1)^{3} \\
&= (x^{6}-1)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 2

Factorise:
(i) $1+64x^{3}$
(ii) $27x^{3}+125y^{3}$
(iii) $(x+1)^{3}+(x-1)^{3}$

#### Solution:

We have:

(i) $1+64x^{3}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (1)^{3}+(4x)^{3} \\
&= (1+4x)(1^{2} - 1 \cdot 4x + (4x)^{2}) \\
&= (1+4x)(1-4x+16x^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) $27x^{3}+125y^{3}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (3x)^{3}+(5y)^{3} \\
&= (3x+5y)((3x)^{2} - 3x \cdot 5y + (5y)^{2}) \\
&= (3x+5y)(9x^{2}-15xy+25y^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

(iii) $(x+1)^{3}+(x-1)^{3}$
Putting $a = (x+1)$ and $b = (x-1)$, we get $a^{3}+b^{3}$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (a+b)(a^{2}-ab+b^{2}) \\
&= \{(x+1)+(x-1)\} \times \{(x+1)^{2}-(x+1)(x-1)+(x-1)^{2}\} \\
&= 2x \times \{(x^{2}+2x+1)-(x^{2}-1)+(x^{2}-2x+1)\} \\
&= 2x \times (x^{2}+2x+1-x^{2}+1+x^{2}-2x+1) \\
&= 2x(x^{2}+3)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 3

Factorise:
(i) $a^{3}-2\sqrt{2}b^{3}$
(ii) $1-64a^{3}$
(iii) $8a^{3}-27b^{3}$

#### Solution:

We have:

(i) $a^{3}-2\sqrt{2}b^{3}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (a)^{3}-(\sqrt{2}b)^{3} \\
&= (a-\sqrt{2}b)(a^{2}+a(\sqrt{2}b)+(\sqrt{2}b)^{2}) \\
&= (a-\sqrt{2}b)(a^{2}+\sqrt{2}ab+2b^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) $1-64a^{3}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (1)^{3}-(4a)^{3} \\
&= (1-4a)(1^{2}+1(4a)+(4a)^{2}) \\
&= (1-4a)(1+4a+16a^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

(iii) $8a^{3}-27b^{3}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (2a)^{3}-(3b)^{3} \\
&= (2a-3b)((2a)^{2}+(2a)(3b)+(3b)^{2}) \\
&= (2a-3b)(4a^{2}+6ab+9b^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 4

Factorise:
(i) $2a^{7}-128a$
(ii) $a^{7}+ab^{6}$
(iii) $32a^{3}+108b^{3}$

#### Solution:

We have:

(i) $2a^{7}-128a$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= 2a(a^{6}-64) \\
&= 2a((a^{3})^{2}-8^{2}) \\
&= 2a(a^{3}-8)(a^{3}+8) \\
&= 2a(a^{3}-2^{3})(a^{3}+2^{3}) \\
&= 2a[(a-2)(a^{2}+2a+4)][(a+2)(a^{2}-2a+4)] \\
&= 2a(a-2)(a+2)(a^{2}+2a+4)(a^{2}-2a+4)
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) $a^{7}+ab^{6}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= a(a^{6}+b^{6}) \\
&= a((a^{2})^{3}+(b^{2})^{3}) \\
&= a(a^{2}+b^{2})((a^{2})^{2}-(a^{2})(b^{2})+(b^{2})^{2}) \\
&= a(a^{2}+b^{2})(a^{4}-a^{2}b^{2}+b^{4})
\end{aligned}
$$

(iii) $32a^{3}+108b^{3}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= 4(8a^{3}+27b^{3}) \\
&= 4((2a)^{3}+(3b)^{3}) \\
&= 4(2a+3b)((2a)^{2}-(2a)(3b)+(3b)^{2}) \\
&= 4(2a+3b)(4a^{2}-6ab+9b^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 5

Factorise:
(i) $a^{3}+b^{3}+a+b$
(ii) $a^{3}-b^{3}-a+b$

#### Solution:

We have:

(i) $a^{3}+b^{3}+a+b$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (a^{3}+b^{3})+(a+b) \\
&= (a+b)(a^{2}-ab+b^{2})+(a+b) \\
&= (a+b)((a^{2}-ab+b^{2})+1) \\
&= (a+b)(a^{2}-ab+b^{2}+1)
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) $a^{3}-b^{3}-a+b$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (a^{3}-b^{3})-(a-b) \\
&= (a-b)(a^{2}+ab+b^{2})-(a-b) \\
&= (a-b)((a^{2}+ab+b^{2})-1) \\
&= (a-b)(a^{2}+ab+b^{2}-1)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 6

Factorise $(a^{6}-b^{6})$.

#### Solution:

We have:

$$
\begin{aligned}
& (a^{6}-b^{6}) \\
&= (a^{3})^{2}-(b^{3})^{2} \\
&= (a^{3}-b^{3})(a^{3}+b^{3}) \\
&= (a-b)(a^{2}+ab+b^{2})(a+b)(a^{2}-ab+b^{2}) \\
&= (a-b)(a+b)(a^{2}+ab+b^{2})(a^{2}-ab+b^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 7

Factorise $(x^{6}-7x^{3}-8)$.

#### Solution:

Putting $y = x^{3}$, we get:

$$
\begin{aligned}
& y^{2}-7y-8 \\
&= y^{2}-8y+y-8 \\
&= y(y-8)+1(y-8) \\
&= (y-8)(y+1)
\end{aligned}
$$

Substituting $x^{3}$ back for $y$:

$$
\begin{aligned}
&= (x^{3}-8)(x^{3}+1) \\
&= (x^{3}-2^{3})(x^{3}+1^{3}) \\
&= (x-2)(x^{2}+2x+4)(x+1)(x^{2}-x+1) \\
&= (x-2)(x+1)(x^{2}+2x+4)(x^{2}-x+1)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 8

Factorise $x^{3}+3x^{2}+3x-7$.

#### Solution:

We have:

$$
\begin{aligned}
& x^{3}+3x^{2}+3x-7 \\
&= (x^{3}+3x^{2}+3x+1) - 1 - 7 \quad \textit{(adding and subtracting 1)} \\
&= (x+1)^{3}-8 \\
&= (x+1)^{3}-2^{3} \\
&= ((x+1)-2)((x+1)^{2}+2(x+1)+2^{2}) \\
&= (x-1)(x^{2}+2x+1+2x+2+4) \\
&= (x-1)(x^{2}+4x+7)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example: 9

If $x+y=12$ and $xy=27$, find the value of $(x^{3}+y^{3})$.

#### Solution:

We have:

$$
\begin{aligned}
(x^{3}+y^{3}) &= (x+y)(x^{2}-xy+y^{2}) \\
&= (x+y)((x+y)^{2}-2xy-xy) \\
&= (x+y)((x+y)^{2}-3xy) \\
&= 12 \times ((12)^{2}-3 \times 27) \\
&= 12 \times (144-81) \\
&= 12 \times 63 \\
&= 756
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\therefore (x^{3}+y^{3}) = 756
$$

### Example: 10

Factorise $(2a+3b)^{3}-(2a-3b)^{3}$.

#### Solution:

Let $x = 2a+3b$ and $y = 2a-3b$. The expression is $x^{3}-y^{3}$.
Note that:
$x-y = (2a+3b)-(2a-3b) = 6b$
$x+y = (2a+3b)+(2a-3b) = 4a$
$xy = (2a+3b)(2a-3b) = 4a^{2}-9b^{2}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& (x-y)(x^{2}+xy+y^{2}) \\
&= (x-y)((x+y)^{2}-xy) \\
&= (6b) \times ((4a)^{2}-(4a^{2}-9b^{2})) \\
&= 6b \times (16a^{2}-4a^{2}+9b^{2}) \\
&= 6b \times (12a^{2}+9b^{2}) \\
&= 18b(4a^{2}+3b^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$
