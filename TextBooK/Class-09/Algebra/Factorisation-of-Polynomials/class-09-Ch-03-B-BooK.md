## Factorising the Difference of Two Squares

The **difference of two squares** formula is:
$$
(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)
$$

---

## Solved Examples

### Example 1

**Factorise:**
1. $(x^{2}-\frac{y^{2}}{100})$
2. $(100-9 x^{2})$
3. $(49 x^{2}-\frac{1}{4})$
#### Solution

We have:

1. **(i)**
   $$ 
   \begin{aligned}
   \left(x^{2}-\frac{y^{2}}{100}\right) 
        &= x^{2}-\left(\frac{y}{10}\right)^{2} \\
        &= \left(x-\frac{y}{10}\right)\left(x+\frac{y}{10}\right) 
        \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]}
   \end{aligned}
   $$

2. **(ii)**
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   \left(100-9x^{2}\right) 
        &= 10^{2}-(3x)^{2} \\
        &= (10-3x)(10+3x) 
        \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]}
   \end{aligned}
   $$

3. **(iii)**
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   \left(49x^{2}-\frac14\right) 
        &= (7x)^{2}-\left(\frac12\right)^{2} \\
        &= \left(7x-\frac12\right)\left(7x+\frac12\right) 
        \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]}\\[4pt]
   \therefore\;
   \left(49x^{2}-\frac14\right) 
        &= \left(7x-\frac12\right)\left(7x+\frac12\right).
   \end{aligned}
   $$

### Example 2

**Factorise:**  
1. $(x^{3}-x)$  
2. $(18 a^{2} x^{2}-32)$  
3. $(2 a^{5}-32 a)$  

#### Solution

1. **(i)**
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   (x^{3}-x) &= x\,(x^{2}-1) \\
            &= x\,(x-1)(x+1) \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]} \\
   \therefore\;(x^{3}-x) &= x\,(x-1)(x+1)
   \end{aligned}
   $$

2. **(ii)**
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   (18 a^{2} x^{2}-32) &= 2\,(9 a^{2} x^{2}-16) \\
                      &= 2\big[(3 a x)^{2}-4^{2}\big] \\
                      &= 2\,(3 a x-4)(3 a x+4) \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]} \\
   \therefore\;(18 a^{2} x^{2}-32) &= 2\,(3 a x-4)(3 a x+4)
   \end{aligned}
   $$

3. **(iii)**
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   (2 a^{5}-32 a) &= 2a\,(a^{4}-16) \\
                 &= 2a\!\big[(a^{2})^{2}-4^{2}\big] \\
                 &= 2a\,(a^{2}-4)(a^{2}+4) \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]} \\
                 &= 2a\,(a-2)(a+2)(a^{2}+4) \\
   \therefore\;(2 a^{5}-32 a) &= 2a\,(a-2)(a+2)(a^{2}+4)
   \end{aligned}
   $$

### Example 3

**Factorise:**  
1. $x^{2}-1-2a-a^{2}$  
2. $1+2ab-\bigl(a^{2}+b^{2}\bigr)$  

#### Solution

1. **(i)**
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   x^{2}-1-2a-a^{2}
        &= x^{2}-\bigl(1+2a+a^{2}\bigr) \\
        &= x^{2}-(1+a)^{2} \\
        &= \{\,x-(1+a)\,\}\{\,x+(1+a)\,\}
           \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]} \\
        &= (x-1-a)(x+1+a) \\
   \therefore\;(x^{2}-1-2a-a^{2})
        &= (x-1-a)(x+1+a)
   \end{aligned}
   $$

2. **(ii)**
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   1+2ab-\bigl(a^{2}+b^{2}\bigr)
        &= 1-\bigl(a^{2}+b^{2}-2ab\bigr) \\
        &= 1^{2}-(a-b)^{2} \\
        &= \{\,1-(a-b)\,\}\{\,1+(a-b)\,\}
           \quad\text{[by }(a^{2}-b^{2})=(a-b)(a+b)\text{]} \\
        &= (1-a+b)(1+a-b) \\
   \therefore\; \bigl(1+2ab-(a^{2}+b^{2})\bigr)
        &= (1-a+b)(1+a-b)
   \end{aligned}
   $$

### Example 4

**Factorise:**
1. $(x^{4}+4)$
2. $(x^{2}+\frac{4}{x^{2}})$
3. $(x^{4}+\frac{1}{x^{4}}+1)$

#### Solution

We have:

(i) By adding and subtracting $(2 \times x^{2} \times 2)$:
$$
\begin{aligned}
(x^{4}+4) &= \left(x^{2}\right)^{2}+2^{2}+\left(2 \times x^{2} \times 2\right)-\left(2 \times x^{2} \times 2\right) \\
&= \left(x^{2}+2\right)^{2}-4 x^{2} \\
&= \left(x^{2}+2\right)^{2}-(2 x)^{2} \\
&= \left(x^{2}+2-2 x\right)\left(x^{2}+2+2 x\right) \\
&= \left(x^{2}-2 x+2\right)\left(x^{2}+2 x+2\right) \\
\therefore (x^{4}+4) &= \left(x^{2}-2 x+2\right)\left(x^{2}+2 x+2\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii) By adding and subtracting $(2 \times x \times \frac{2}{x})$:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(x^{2}+\frac{4}{x^{2}}\right) &= x^{2}+\left(\frac{2}{x}\right)^{2}+\left(2 \times x \times \frac{2}{x}\right)-\left(2 \times x \times \frac{2}{x}\right) \\
&= \left(x+\frac{2}{x}\right)^{2}-4 \\
&= \left\{\left(x+\frac{2}{x}\right)^{2}-2^{2}\right\} \\
&= \left(x+\frac{2}{x}-2\right)\left(x+\frac{2}{x}+2\right) \\
\therefore \left(x^{2}+\frac{4}{x^{2}}\right) &= \left(x+\frac{2}{x}-2\right)\left(x+\frac{2}{x}+2\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

(iii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(x^{4}+\frac{1}{x^{4}}+1\right) &= \left(x^{4}+\frac{1}{x^{4}}+2\right)-1 \\
&= \left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}\right)^{2}-1^{2} \\
&= \left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}-1\right)\left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}+1\right) \\
&= \left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}-1\right)\left\{\left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}+2\right)-1\right\} \\
&= \left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}-1\right)\left\{\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)^{2}-1^{2}\right\} \\
&= \left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}-1\right)\left(x+\frac{1}{x}-1\right)\left(x+\frac{1}{x}+1\right) \\
\therefore \left(x^{4}+\frac{1}{x^{4}}+1\right) &= \left(x^{2}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}-1\right)\left(x+\frac{1}{x}-1\right)\left(x+\frac{1}{x}+1\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 5

**Factorise:** $(x^{4}+x^{2} y^{2}+y^{4})$

#### Solution

We have:
$$
\begin{aligned}
(x^{4}+x^{2} y^{2}+y^{4}) &= (x^{4}+2 x^{2} y^{2}+y^{4})-x^{2} y^{2} \\
&= (x^{2}+y^{2})^{2}-(x y)^{2} \\
&= (x^{2}+y^{2}-x y)(x^{2}+y^{2}+x y) \\
\therefore (x^{4}+x^{2} y^{2}+y^{4}) &= (x^{2}-xy+y^{2})(x^{2}+xy+y^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 6

**Factorise:** $a(a-1)-b(b-1)$

#### Solution

We have:
$$
\begin{aligned}
a(a-1)-b(b-1) &= a^{2}-a-b^{2}+b \\
&= (a^{2}-b^{2})-(a-b) \\
&= (a-b)(a+b)-(a-b) \\
&= (a-b)\{(a+b)-1\} \\
&= (a-b)(a+b-1)
\end{aligned}
$$
Hence, $a(a-1)-b(b-1)=(a-b)(a+b-1)$.

### Example 7

**Factorise:**
1. $(x^{4}+3 x^{2}+4)$
2. $(x^{4}+5 x^{2}+9)$

#### Solution

We have:

(i)
$$
\begin{aligned}
(x^{4}+3 x^{2}+4) &= (x^{4}+4 x^{2}+4)-x^{2} \\
&= (x^{2}+2)^{2}-x^{2} \\
&= (x^{2}+2-x)(x^{2}+2+x) \\
&= (x^{2}-x+2)(x^{2}+x+2)
\end{aligned}
$$

(ii)
$$
\begin{aligned}
(x^{4}+5 x^{2}+9) &= (x^{4}+6 x^{2}+9)-x^{2} \\
&= (x^{2}+3)^{2}-x^{2} \\
&= (x^{2}+3-x)(x^{2}+3+x) \\
&= (x^{2}-x+3)(x^{2}+x+3) \\
\therefore (x^{4}+5 x^{2} + 9) &= (x^{2}-x+3)(x^{2}+x+3)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 8

**Factorise:** $(x^{8}-y^{8})$

#### Solution

We have:
$$
\begin{aligned}
(x^{8}-y^{8}) &= (x^{4})^{2}-(y^{4})^{2} \\
&= (x^{4}-y^{4})(x^{4}+y^{4}) \\
&= \left\{\left(x^{2}\right)^{2}-\left(y^{2}\right)^{2}\right\}\left(x^{4}+y^{4}+2 x^{2} y^{2}-2 x^{2} y^{2}\right) \\
&= (x^{2}-y^{2})(x^{2}+y^{2})\left[\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{2}-(\sqrt{2} x y)^{2}\right] \\
&= (x-y)(x+y)(x^{2}+y^{2})(x^{2}+y^{2}-\sqrt{2} x y)(x^{2}+y^{2}+\sqrt{2} x y) \\
\therefore (x^{8}-y^{8}) &= (x-y)(x+y)(x^{2}+y^{2})(x^{2}-\sqrt{2}xy+y^{2})(x^{2}+\sqrt{2}xy+y^{2})
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 9

**Factorise:** $\left(2 x+\frac{1}{3}\right)^{2}-\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)^{2}$

#### Solution

We have:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(2 x+\frac{1}{3}\right)^{2}-\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)^{2} &= \left\{\left(2 x+\frac{1}{3}\right)+\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)\right\}\left\{\left(2 x+\frac{1}{3}\right)-\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)\right\} \\
&= \left(2 x+\frac{1}{3}+x-\frac{1}{2}\right)\left(2 x+\frac{1}{3}-x+\frac{1}{2}\right) \\
&= \left\{3 x+\left(\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{2}\right)\right\}\left\{x+\left(\frac{1}{3}+\frac{1}{2}\right)\right\} \\
&= \left(3 x-\frac{1}{6}\right)\left(x+\frac{5}{6}\right) \\
\therefore \left(2 x+\frac{1}{3}\right)^{2}-\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)^{2} &= \left(3 x-\frac{1}{6}\right)\left(x+\frac{5}{6}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$
