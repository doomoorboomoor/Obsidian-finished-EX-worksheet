### Answer to Question 01

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-6/Chapter-16/Section-B/Q-01

- Answer:

44 cm

> [!INFO]
>
> $(2 \pi R-R)=37 \Rightarrow R(2 \pi-1)=37$
> 
> $$
> \Rightarrow R\left(2 \times \frac{22}{7}-1\right)=37 \Rightarrow R=7 \mathrm{~cm} .
> $$
> 
> $\therefore \quad$ circumference $=2 \pi R=\left(2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 7\right) \mathrm{cm}=44 \mathrm{~cm}$.
