### Answer to Question 26

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-5/Chapter-11/Section-A/Q-26

- Answer:

$\frac{1}{3}$

> [!INFO]
>
> Adding, we get $3\left(x+\frac{1}{x}\right)=(\operatorname{cosec} \theta+\cot \theta)$.
> Subtracting, we get $3\left(x-\frac{1}{x}\right)=(\operatorname{cosec} \theta-\cot \theta)$.
> $\therefore \quad 9\left(x^{2}-\frac{1}{x^{2}}\right)=\left(\operatorname{cosec}^{2} \theta-\cot ^{2} \theta\right)=1 \Rightarrow 3\left(x^{2}-\frac{1}{x^{2}}\right)=\frac{1}{3}$.
