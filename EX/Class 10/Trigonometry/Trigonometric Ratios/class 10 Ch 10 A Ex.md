### Answer to Question 06

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-5/Chapter-10/Section-A/Q-06

- Answer:

$\sin \theta=\frac{a^{2}-b^{2}}{a^{2}+b^{2}}, \cos \theta=\frac{2 a b}{a^{2}+b^{2}}, \tan \theta=\frac{a^{2}-b^{2}}{2 a b}, \operatorname{cosec} \theta=\frac{a^{2}+b^{2}}{a^{2}-b^{2}}, \sec \theta=\frac{a^{2}+b^{2}}{2 a b}, \cot \theta=\frac{2 a b}{a^{2}-b^{2}}$

> [!INFO]
>
> In right $\triangle A B C, \angle B=90^{\circ}$ and $\angle B A C=\theta$.
> 
> Given: $\sin \theta=\frac{a^{2}-b^{2}}{a^{2}+b^{2}}=\frac{B C}{A C}$.
> Let $B C=\left(a^{2}-b^{2}\right)$ and $A C=\left(a^{2}+b^{2}\right)$. Then,
> 
> $$
> A B^{2}=\left(A C^{2}-B C^{2}\right)=\left(a^{2}+b^{2}\right)^{2}-\left(a^{2}-b^{2}\right)^{2}=4 a^{2} b^{2}
> $$
> 
> $\Rightarrow A B=2 a b$.
> Now, we can find all the T-ratios of $\theta$.

- Answer:

$\sin A=\frac{15}{17}, \sec A=\frac{17}{8}$

> [!INFO]
>
> $\cot A=\frac{8}{15} \Rightarrow \tan A=\frac{15}{8}=\frac{B C}{A B}$.
> $\therefore \quad A C^{2}=A B^{2}+B C^{2}=(8)^{2}+(15)^{2}=64+225=289$
> $\Rightarrow \quad A C=\sqrt{289}=17$.
> $\therefore \quad \sin A=\frac{B C}{A C}=\frac{15}{17}$ and $\sec A=\frac{A C}{A B}=\frac{17}{8}$.
