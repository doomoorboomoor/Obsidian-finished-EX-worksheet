> [!INFO]
>
> Let $A B$ be the stick and $A C$ be its shadow.
> 
> Let $D E$ be the tree and $D F$ be its shadow.
> $\triangle A B C \sim \triangle D E F$.
> $\therefore \quad \frac{A B}{D E}=\frac{A C}{D F} \Rightarrow \frac{5}{12.5}=\frac{2}{x}$
> $\Rightarrow \quad x=\left(\frac{12.5 \times 2}{5}\right)=5$.
