### Answer to Question 09

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-4/Chapter-07/Section-C/Q-09

- Answer:

> [!INFO]
>
> $\frac{A P}{A B}=\frac{1}{(1+3)}=\frac{1}{4}, \frac{A Q}{A C}=\frac{1.5}{(1.5+4.5)}=\frac{1.5}{6}=\frac{1}{4}$.
>
> $\therefore \quad \triangle A P Q \sim \triangle A B C \quad$ [by SAS-similarity]
>
> $\Rightarrow \quad \frac{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A P Q)}{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A B C)}=\frac{A P^{2}}{A B^{2}}=\left(\frac{A P}{A B}\right)^{2}=\left(\frac{1}{4}\right)^{2}=\frac{1}{16}$
>
> $\Rightarrow \quad \operatorname{ar}(\triangle A P Q)=\frac{1}{16} \cdot \operatorname{ar}(\triangle A B C)$.

### Answer to Question 11

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-4/Chapter-07/Section-C/Q-11

- Answer:

$169 : 25$

> [!INFO]
>
> In $\triangle B A C$ and $\triangle A D C$, we have
>
> $\begin{aligned} \angle B A C & =\angle A D C=90^{\circ} \\ \text{ and } \angle A C B & =\angle D C A=\angle C .\end{aligned}$
>
> $\therefore \quad \triangle B A C \sim \triangle A D C$. [by AA-similarity].
>
> $\therefore \quad \frac{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A B C)}{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A D C)}=\frac{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle B A C)}{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A D C)}=\frac{B C^{2}}{A C^{2}}$.

### Answer to Question 12

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-4/Chapter-07/Section-C/Q-12

- Answer:

$9 : 16$

> [!INFO]
>
> $\triangle A D E \sim \triangle A B C$.
>
> $\therefore \quad \frac{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A D E)}{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A B C)}=\frac{D E^{2}}{B C^{2}}=\left(\frac{D E}{B C}\right)^{2}=\left(\frac{3}{5}\right)^{2}=\frac{9}{25}$.
>
> Let $\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A D E)=9 x$ sq units.
>
> Then, $\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A B C)=25 x$ sq units.
>
> $\therefore \quad \operatorname{ar}(\text{trap. } B C E D)=\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A B C)-\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A D E)$
> $= (25 x-9 x) \text{ sq units} = (16 x) \text{ sq units}$.
>
> $\therefore \quad \frac{\operatorname{ar}(\triangle A D E)}{\operatorname{ar}(\text{trap. } B C E D)}=\frac{9 x}{16 x}=\frac{9}{16}$.
