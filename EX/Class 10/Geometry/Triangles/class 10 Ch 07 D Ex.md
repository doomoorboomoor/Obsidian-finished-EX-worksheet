- Answer:

>
> In $\triangle A E B, \angle A E B=90^{\circ}$.
>
> $$
> \begin{align*}

> & \text { In } \triangle A E D, \angle A E D=90^{\circ} . \\
> & \therefore \quad A D^{2}=\left(A E^{2}+D E^{2}\right) \\

> & =\left(A D^{2}-D E^{2}\right)+\left(\frac{1}{2} B C-D E\right)^{2} \\

> \end{align*}
> $$
