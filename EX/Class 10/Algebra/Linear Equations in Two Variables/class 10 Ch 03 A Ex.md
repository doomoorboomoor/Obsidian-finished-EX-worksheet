### Answer to Question 29

- [ ] InBox/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-2/Chapter-03/Section-A/Q-29

- Answer:

8 sq units

> [!INFO]
>
> The line $2 x+y=2$ cuts the $x$-axis at $A(1,0)$ and the $y$-axis at $B(0,2)$.
>
> The line $2 x+y=6$ cuts the $x$-axis at $C(3,0)$ and the $y$-axis at $D(0,6)$.
>
> Area of trap. $A B D C=\operatorname{ar}(\triangle O C D)-\operatorname{ar}(\triangle O A B)$
>
> $$
> =\left(\frac{1}{2} \times 3 \times 6\right)-\left(\frac{1}{2} \times 1 \times 2\right)=8 \text{ sq units.}
> $$
