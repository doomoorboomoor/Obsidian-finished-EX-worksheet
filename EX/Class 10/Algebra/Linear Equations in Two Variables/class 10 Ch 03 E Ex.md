### Answer to Question 20

- [ ] InBox/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-2/Chapter-03/Section-D/Q-20

- Answer:

$k=6$

> [!INFO]
>
> $\frac{k-3}{k}=\frac{3}{k}=\frac{-k}{-12} \Rightarrow \frac{k-3}{k}=\frac{3}{k}$ and $\frac{3}{k}=\frac{k}{12}$.
>
> $\therefore \quad k^{2}-6 k=0$ and $k^{2}=36 \Rightarrow k(k-6)=0$ and $k^{2}=36$
> $\Rightarrow \quad(k=0$ or $k=6)$ and ( $k=6$ or $k=-6$ )
> $\Rightarrow \quad k=6$.
