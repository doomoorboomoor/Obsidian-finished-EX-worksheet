### Answer to Question 21/i

- [ ] InBox/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-2/Chapter-04/Section-D/Q-21/Q-i

- Answer:

> [!INFO]
>
> $4(a c+b d)^{2}-4\left(a^{2}+b^{2}\right)\left(c^{2}+d^{2}\right)=0$
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & \Rightarrow \quad\left(a^{2}+b^{2}\right)\left(c^{2}+d^{2}\right)-(a c+b d)^{2}=0 \\
> & \Rightarrow \quad a^{2} d^{2}+b^{2} c^{2}-2 a b c d=0 \Rightarrow(a d-b c)^{2}=0 \Rightarrow a d-b c=0 \\
> & \Rightarrow \quad a d=b c \Rightarrow \frac{a}{b}=\frac{c}{d} .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
