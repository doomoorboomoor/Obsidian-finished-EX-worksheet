### Answer to Question 02/i

- [ ] InBox/Ans/Ex/Class-10/Unit-2/Chapter-05/Section-C/Q-02/Q-i

- Answer:

$1046 \frac{1}{2}$

> [!INFO]
>
> $a=7, d=\left(\frac{21}{2}-7\right)=\frac{7}{2}$ and $l=84$.
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
