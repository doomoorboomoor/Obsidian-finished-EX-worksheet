### Answer to Question 01

- Answer:









> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> (i) $f\left(x_{1}\right)=f\left(x_{2}\right) \Rightarrow 2 x_{1}=2 x_{2} \Rightarrow x_{1}=x_{2}$. So, $f$ is one-one.
> 
> (ii) Let $y=2 x$. Then, $x=\frac{1}{2} y$.
> 
> Thus for each $y$ in codomain $R$, there exists $\frac{1}{2} y$ such that
> 
> $$

> $$
> 
> $\therefore f$ is onto.
