### Answer to Question 09

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-1/Chapter-02/Section-B/Q-09

- Answer:

$g: Z \rightarrow Z: g(y)=\frac{1}{2} y$

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let $x \in Z$. Then,
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> (g \circ f)=I_{Z} & \Rightarrow(g \circ f)(x)=I_{Z}(x) \\
> & \Rightarrow g[f(x)]=x \\
> & \Rightarrow g(2 x)=x \\
> & \Rightarrow g(y)=\frac{1}{2} y \quad[\text{where } 2 x=y].
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
> Thus, $g: Z \rightarrow Z: g(y)=\frac{1}{2} y$.
>
