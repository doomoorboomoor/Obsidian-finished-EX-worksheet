### Answer to Question 10

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-1/Chapter-01/Section-A/Q-10

- Answer:



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let $O$ be the origin and let $P, Q, X$ be any three points in a plane.
> 
> Then, (i) $O P=O P$ is always true. So, $R$ is reflextive.
> (ii) $O P=O Q \Rightarrow O Q=O P$. So, $R$ is symmetric.
> (iii) $(O P=O Q$ and $O Q=O X) \Rightarrow(O P=O X)$. So, $R$ transitive.
> 
> Hence, $R$ is an equivalence relation.
>
