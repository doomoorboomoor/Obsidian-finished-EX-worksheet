### Answer to Question 01

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-1/Chapter-04/Section-Q/Q-01

(a)

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let $\cos ^{-1}\left(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)=x$, where $x \in[0, \pi]$.

> Then, $\cos x=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}=\cos \frac{\pi}{6} \Rightarrow x=\frac{\pi}{6}$.
