### Answer to Question 04/iv

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-1/Chapter-04/Section-C/Q-04/Q-iv

- Answer:






> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Putting $x=\cot \theta$, we get:
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \cot ^{-1}\left(\sqrt{1+x^{2}}-x\right) & =\cot ^{-1}(\operatorname{cosec} \theta-\cot \theta) \\
> & =\cot ^{-1}\left(\frac{1-\cos \theta}{\sin \theta}\right)=\cot ^{-1}\left(\frac{2 \sin ^{2} \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 \sin \frac{\theta}{2} \cos \frac{\theta}{2}}\right) \\
> & =\cot ^{-1}\left(\tan \frac{\theta}{2}\right)=\cot ^{-1}\left[\cot \left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\frac{\theta}{2}\right)\right] \\
> & =\frac{\pi}{2}-\frac{\theta}{2}=\left(\frac{\pi}{2}-\frac{1}{2} \cot ^{-1} x\right).
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
>
