### Answer to Question 14



- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-3/Chapter-18/Section-B/Q-14



- Answer:







> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let $A x^{2}+B y^{2}=1$ be given. Then,
>
> $$
> \begin{align*}
> 2 A x+2 B y \frac{d y}{d x}=0 & \Rightarrow A x+B y \frac{d y}{d x}=0  \tag{i}\\
> & \Rightarrow A x=-B y \frac{d y}{d x} \Rightarrow \frac{A}{B}=\frac{-y}{x} \cdot \frac{d y}{d x} \tag{ii}
> \end{align*}
> $$
>
> On differentiating (i) w.r.t. $x$, we get
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> A+B\left[y \frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}+\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)^{2}\right]=0 & \Rightarrow y \frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}+\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)^{2}=\frac{-A}{B} \\
> & \Rightarrow\left\{y \frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}+\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)^{2}\right\}=\frac{y}{x} \cdot \frac{d y}{d x} \quad \text{[from (ii)].}
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
>

### Answer to Question 15



- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-3/Chapter-18/Section-B/Q-15



- Answer:







> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let $c=\tan A$ and $x=\tan B$. Then,
>
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> y=\frac{\tan A-\tan B}{1+\tan A \tan B}=\tan (A-B) & \Rightarrow \tan ^{-1} y=A-B \\
> & \Rightarrow \tan ^{-1} y=\tan ^{-1}(c)-\tan ^{-1} x \\
> & \Rightarrow \frac{1}{\left(1+y^{2}\right)} \frac{d y}{d x}=0-\frac{1}{\left(1+x^{2}\right)} \\
> & \Rightarrow\left(1+x^{2}\right) \frac{d y}{d x}+\left(1+y^{2}\right)=0
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
>

### Answer to Question 16



- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-3/Chapter-18/Section-B/Q-16



- Answer:







> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> $y=\log \left(x+\sqrt{x^{2}+a^{2}}\right)$
>
> $$
> \begin{gathered}
> \Rightarrow \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{1}{\left(x+\sqrt{x^{2}+a^{2}}\right)} \cdot\left\{1+\frac{2 x}{2 \sqrt{x^{2}+a^{2}}}\right\} \Rightarrow y_{1}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^{2}+a^{2}}} \\
> \Rightarrow y_{1}^{2}\left(x^{2}+a^{2}\right)=1 \Rightarrow y_{1}^{2}(2 x)+\left(x^{2}+a^{2}\right) 2 y_{1} y_{2}=0 \\
> \Rightarrow\left(x^{2}+a^{2}\right) y_{2}+x y_{1}=0
> \end{gathered}
> $$
>
