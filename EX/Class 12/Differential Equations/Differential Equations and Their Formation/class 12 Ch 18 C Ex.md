### Answer to Question 08

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-3/Chapter-18/Section-C/Q-08

- Answer:

$p^{2}\left(x^{2}-2 y^{2}\right)-4 p x y-x^{2}=0$, where $p=\frac{d y}{d x}$



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Given family of curves is $x^{2}+y^{2}-2 a y=a^{2}$.
> 
> Differentiating (i) w.r.t. $x$, we get
> 
> $$
> \begin{align*}
> 2 x+2 y \frac{d y}{d x}-2 a \frac{d y}{d x}=0 & \Rightarrow x+y \frac{d y}{d x}=a \frac{d y}{d x}  \tag{i}\\
> & \Rightarrow a=\frac{x+y p}{p}, \text { where } p=\frac{d y}{d x} .
> \end{align*}
> $$
> 
> Putting $a=\frac{x+y p}{p}$ in (i), we get:
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & x^{2}+y^{2}-\frac{2(x+y p) y}{p}=\left(\frac{x+y p}{p}\right)^{2} \\
> \Rightarrow & \left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right) p^{2}-2 y p(x+y p)=(x+y p)^{2} \\
> \Rightarrow & x^{2} p^{2}+y^{2} p^{2}-2 x y p-2 y^{2} p^{2}=x^{2}+y^{2} p^{2}+2 x y p \\
> \Rightarrow & x^{2} p^{2}-2 y^{2} p^{2}-4 x y p-x^{2}=0 \\
> \Rightarrow & \left(x^{2}-2 y^{2}\right) p^{2}-4 x y p-x^{2}=0, \text { where } p=\frac{d y}{d x} .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
>

### Answer to Question 13

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-3/Chapter-18/Section-C/Q-13

- Answer:

$\left(x^{2}-y^{2}-a^{2}\right) \frac{d y}{d x}=2 x y$



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> The general equation of a circle is
> 
> $$
> \begin{equation*}
> x^{2}+y^{2}+2 g x+2 f y+c=0 \tag{i}
> \end{equation*}
> $$
> 
> If it passes through the points $A(-a, 0)$ and $B(a, 0)$, we have
> 
> $$
> \begin{equation*}
> a^{2}-2 g a+c=0 \quad \ldots \text { (ii) and } a^{2}+2 g a+c=0 \tag{iii}
> \end{equation*}
> $$
> 
> Adding (ii) and (iii), we get $2\left(a^{2}+c\right)=0 \Rightarrow a^{2}+c=0 \Rightarrow c=-a^{2}$.
> 
> Putting $c=-a^{2}$ in (iii), we get $2 g a=0 \Rightarrow g=0$.
> 
> Putting $g=0$ and $c=-a^{2}$ in (i), we get
> 
> $x^{2}+y^{2}+2 f y-a^{2}=0 \quad \ldots$ (iv), where $f$ is the parameter.
> 
> Differentiating (iv) w.r.t. $x$, we get
> 
> $$
> 2 x+2 y y_{1}+2 f y_{1}=0 \Rightarrow f y_{1}=-\left(x+y y_{1}\right) \Rightarrow f=\frac{-\left(x+y y_{1}\right)}{y_{1}}
> $$
> 
> Putting this value of $f$ in (iv), we get
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & x^{2}+y^{2}-\frac{2 y\left(x+y y_{1}\right)}{y_{1}}-a^{2}=0 \\
> \Rightarrow & x^{2} y_{1}+y^{2} y_{1}-2 x y-2 y^{2} y_{1}-a^{2} y_{1}=0 \Rightarrow\left(x^{2}-y^{2}-a^{2}\right) y_{1}=2 x y .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
>

### Answer to Question 15

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-3/Chapter-18/Section-C/Q-15

- Answer:

$x y\left(\frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}\right)+x\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)^{2}-y \frac{d y}{d x}=0$



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> The equation of the family of ellipses having centre at the origin and foci on the $y$-axis, is given by
> 
> $$
> \frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}}+\frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}}=1 \quad \ldots(\mathrm{i}), \text { where } b>a \text { and } a, b \text { are the parameters. }
> $$
> 
> Differentiating (i), w.r.t. $x$, we get
> 
> $$
> \begin{equation*}
> \frac{2 x}{a^{2}}+\frac{2 y}{b^{2}} \frac{d y}{d x}=0 \Rightarrow \frac{x}{a^{2}}+\frac{y y_{1}}{b^{2}}=0 \tag{ii}
> \end{equation*}
> $$
> 
> Differentiating (ii) w.r.t. $x$, we get
> 
> $$
> \begin{equation*}
> \frac{1}{a^{2}}+\frac{y y_{2}}{b^{2}}+\frac{y_{1}^{2}}{b^{2}}=0 \Rightarrow \frac{x}{a^{2}}+\frac{x y y_{2}}{b^{2}}+\frac{x y_{1}^{2}}{b^{2}}=0 . \tag{iii}
> \end{equation*}
> $$
> 
> Subtracting (ii) from (iii), we get
> 
> $$
> \frac{1}{b^{2}}\left\{x y y_{2}+x y_{1}^{2}-y y_{1}\right\}=0 \Rightarrow x y\left(\frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}\right)+x\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)^{2}-y\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)=0 .
> $$
> 
>

### Answer to Question 16

- Answer:

$x y \frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}+x\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)^{2}-y \frac{d y}{d x}=0$



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> The equation of the family of hyperbolas having foci on the $x$-axis and centre at the origin is given by
> 
> $$
> \frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}}-\frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}}=1 \quad \ldots \text { (i), where } a \text { and } b \text { are the parameters. }
> $$
> 
> Differentiating (i), w.r.t. $x$, we get
> 
> $$
> \begin{equation*}
> \frac{2 x}{a^{2}}-\frac{2 y y_{1}}{b^{2}}=0 \Rightarrow \frac{x}{a^{2}}-\frac{y y_{1}}{b^{2}}=0 \tag{ii}
> \end{equation*}
> $$
> 
> Differentiating (ii), w.r.t. $x$, we get
> 
> $$
> \begin{equation*}
> \frac{1}{a^{2}}-\frac{y y_{2}}{b^{2}}-\frac{y_{1}^{2}}{b^{2}}=0 \tag{iii}
> \end{equation*}
> $$
> 
> Multiplying (iii) by $x$ and subtracting from (ii), we get
> 
> $$
> \frac{1}{b^{2}}\left\{x y y_{2}+x y_{1}^{2}-y y_{1}\right\}=0 \Rightarrow x y\left(\frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}\right)+x\left(\frac{d y}{d x}\right)^{2}-y \frac{d y}{d x}=0 .
> $$
> 
>
