> $\therefore \quad \sin \theta=\frac{(2 \times 1)+(1 \times 1)+(-2) \times 0}{\left\{\sqrt{2^{2}+1^{2}+(-2)^{2}}\right\}\left\{\sqrt{1^{2}+1^{2}+0^{2}}\right\}}=\frac{(2+1+0)}{(\sqrt{9})(\sqrt{2})} =\frac{3}{3 \sqrt{2}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \Rightarrow \theta=\frac{\pi}{4}$.
>
