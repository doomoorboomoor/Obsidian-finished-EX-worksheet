### Answer to Question 02

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-4/Chapter-28/Section-D/Q-02

- Answer:

$\vec{r} \cdot(2 \hat{i}-3 \hat{j}+5 \hat{k})+11=0$

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let the required plane be $x-2 y+2 z+k=0$ for some constants $k$.
> 
> Then, its distance from the point $P(1,2,3)$ is
> 
> $$
> \frac{|1-2 \times 2+2 \times 3+k|}{\sqrt{1^{2}+(-2)^{2}+2^{2}}}=\frac{|3+k|}{3}=1 \Rightarrow|3+k|=3
> $$
> 
> $\Rightarrow \quad 3+k=3$ or $3+k=-3 \Rightarrow k=0$ or $k=-6$.
> Hence, the required equations are $x-2 y+2 z=0$ or $x-2 y+2 z-6=0$.
>

### Answer to Question 09

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-4/Chapter-28/Section-D/Q-09

- Answer:

$\frac{7}{\sqrt{56}}$ units

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let $P\left(x_{1}, y_{1} z_{1}\right)$ be any point on the plane $x+2 y+3 z+7=0$.
> 
> Then, $x_{1}+2 y_{1}+3 z_{1}=-7$.
> 
> $$
> \begin{align*}
> \therefore \quad p & =\frac{\left|2 x_{1}+4 y_{1}+6 z_{1}+7\right|}{\sqrt{2^{2}+4^{2}+6^{2}}}=\frac{\left|2\left(x_{1}+2 y_{1}+3 z_{1}\right)+7\right|}{\sqrt{56}}  \tag{i}\\
> & =\frac{|2 \times(-7)+7|}{\sqrt{56}}=\frac{7}{\sqrt{56}} \text { units. }
> \end{align*}
> $$
>
