### Answer to Question 37/i

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-12/Unit-2/Chapter-07/Section-A/Q-37/Q-i

- Answer:



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> (i) $|I|=1$. Cofactors of $a_{i j}$ in $|I|$ are $A_{i j}=\left\{\begin{array}{l}1, \text{ when } i=j \\ 0, \text{ when } i \neq j\end{array}\right.$
> $$\therefore \quad(\operatorname{adj} I)=\left[A_{i j}\right]^{\prime}=I^{\prime}=I .$$
> 
>
