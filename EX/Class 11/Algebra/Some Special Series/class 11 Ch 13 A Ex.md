### Answer to Question 17

$\frac{n}{(n+1)}$







> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> $T_{n}=\frac{1}{n(n+1)}=\frac{A}{n}+\frac{B}{n+1}=\frac{A(n+1)+B n}{n(n+1)}=\frac{(A+B) n+A}{n(n+1)}$.
> 
> $\therefore \quad A+B=0$ and $A=1 \Rightarrow A=1$ and $B=-1$
> 
> $\therefore \quad T_{n}=\left(\frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{n+1}\right)$
> 
> $\therefore \quad T_{1}=\left(\frac{1}{1}-\frac{1}{2}\right)$
> 
> $T_{2}=\left(\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{3}\right)$
> 
> $T_{3}=\left(\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{4}\right)$
> 
> ... ... ... ...
> 
> ... ... ... ...
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & T_{n-1}=\left(\frac{1}{n-1}-\frac{1}{n}\right) \\
> & T_{n}=\left(\frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{n+1}\right) .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
> Adding columnwise, we get
> 
> $$
> S_{n}=\left(T_{1}+T_{2}+T_{3}+\ldots+T_{n}\right)=\left(1-\frac{1}{n+1}\right)=\frac{(n+1)-1}{n+1}=\frac{n}{n+1} .
> $$
> 
>

### Answer to Question 18

$\frac{n}{(2 n+1)}$







> [!INFO] Hint
>

> 
> $$
> \begin{array}{ll}
> \therefore & 2(A+B)=0 \text{ and } A-B=1 \Rightarrow A+B=0, A-B=1 \Rightarrow A=\frac{1}{2}, B=\frac{-1}{2} . \\
> \therefore & T_{n}=\frac{1}{2} \times \left\{\frac{1}{2 n-1}-\frac{1}{2 n+1}\right\} . \\
> \therefore & T_{1}=\frac{1}{2} \times \left(\frac{1}{1}-\frac{1}{3}\right) \\
> & T_{2}=\frac{1}{2} \times \left(\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{5}\right) \\

> & T_{n}=\frac{1}{2} \times \left\{\frac{1}{2 n-1}-\frac{1}{2 n+1}\right\} .
> \end{array}
> $$

> $$
