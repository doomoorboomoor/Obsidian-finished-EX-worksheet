- Answer:

> [!INFO] Hint

> 
> $$

> \therefore & S=\frac{a\left(r^{n}-1\right)}{(r-1)}=\frac{\sqrt{2}\left\{(\sqrt{3})^{10}-1\right\}}{(\sqrt{3}-1)} \times \frac{(\sqrt{3}+1)}{(\sqrt{3}+1)}=\frac{\sqrt{2}(243-1)(\sqrt{3}+1)}{2} \\
> \Rightarrow & S=121(\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}) .
> \end{array}
> $$
