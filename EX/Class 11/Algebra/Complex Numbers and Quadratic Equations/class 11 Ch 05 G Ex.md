### Answer to Question 10

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-2/Chapter-05/Section-G/Q-10

- Answer:

$n=4$



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> $\left(\frac{1+i}{1-i}\right)=\frac{(1+i)}{(1-i)} \times \frac{(1+i)}{(1+i)}=\frac{(1+i)^{2}}{\left(1-i^{2}\right)}=\frac{\left(1+i^{2}+2 i\right)}{2}=\frac{2 i}{2}=i$.

> $$
