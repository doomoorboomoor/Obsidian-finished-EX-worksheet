> [!INFO] Hint
>

> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}

> & \Rightarrow \quad u=x^{3}-3 x^{2} y \text { and } v=3 x^{2} y-y^{3} \\
> & \Rightarrow \quad\left(\frac{u}{x}+\frac{v}{y}\right)=\left(x^{2}-3 y^{2}\right)+\left(3 x^{2}-y^{2}\right)=4\left(x^{2}-y^{2}\right)
> \end{aligned}
> $$

### Answer to Question 12



- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-2/Chapter-05/Section-C/Q-12



- Answer:











> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> We have
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \frac{1-i x}{1+i x}=\frac{a-i b}{1} & \Rightarrow \frac{(1-i x)+(1+i x)}{(1-i x)-(1+i x)}=\frac{(a-i b)+1}{(a-i b)-1} \text { [by componendo and dividendo] } \\
> & \Rightarrow \frac{2}{-2 i x}=\frac{(a+1)-i b}{(a-1)-i b} \Rightarrow-i x=\frac{(a-1)-i b}{(a+1)-i b} \times \frac{(a+1)+i b}{(a+1)+i b} \\
> & \Rightarrow-i x=\frac{\left(a^{2}-1+b^{2}\right)+\{(a-1) b-(a+1) b\} i}{(a+1)^{2}+b^{2}} \\
> & =\frac{-2 b i}{(a+1)^{2}+b^{2}} \\
> & \Rightarrow x=\frac{2 b}{(a+1)^{2}+b^{2}}, \text { which is purely real. }
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
>
