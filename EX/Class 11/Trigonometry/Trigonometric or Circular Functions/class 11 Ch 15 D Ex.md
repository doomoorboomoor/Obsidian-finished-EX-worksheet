### Answer to Question 17

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-1/Chapter-15/Section-D/Q-17

- Answer:



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> $\mathrm{LHS} =\frac{1}{2 \sin x}(2 \sin x \cos x) \cos 2 x \cos 4 x \cos 8 x$
> 
> $$
\begin{aligned}
& =\frac{1}{2 \sin x}(\sin 2 x \cos 2 x) \cos 4 x \cos 8 x \\
& =\frac{1}{4 \sin x}(2 \sin 2 x \cos 2 x) \cos 4 x \cos 8 x \\
& =\frac{1}{4 \sin x}(\sin 4 x \cos 4 x) \cos 8 x \\
& =\frac{1}{8 \sin x}(2 \sin 4 x \cos 4 x) \cos 8 x \\
& =\frac{1}{8 \sin x}(\sin 8 x \cos 8 x) \\
& =\frac{1}{16 \sin x}(2 \sin 8 x \cos 8 x) \\
& =\frac{1}{16 \sin x}(\sin 16 x)=\frac{\sin 16 x}{16 \sin x}=\mathrm{RHS}
\end{aligned}
$$
> 
>
