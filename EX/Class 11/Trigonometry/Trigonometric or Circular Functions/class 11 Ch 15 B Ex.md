### Answer to Question 18/i

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-1/Chapter-15/Section-B/Q-18/Q-i

- Answer:

$\frac{3}{5}$



> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> In the 4th quadrant, $\cos x>0, \sin x<0$ and $\tan x<0$.
>
> In the 3rd quadrant, $\tan y>0, \sin y<0$ and $\cos y<0$.
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & \therefore \quad \sin x=-\sqrt{1-\frac{9}{25}}=-\sqrt{\frac{16}{25}}=\frac{-4}{5}, \\
> & \tan x=\left(\frac{-4}{5} \times \frac{5}{3}\right)=\frac{-4}{3} \\
> & \cos y=\frac{-24}{25}, \sin y=-\sqrt{1-\frac{576}{625}}=-\sqrt{\frac{49}{625}}=\frac{-7}{25} \text{ and } \tan y=\frac{7}{24} .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
> 
> Now, find the required values.
> 
>
