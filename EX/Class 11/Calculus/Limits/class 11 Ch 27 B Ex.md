### Answer to Question 09

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-4/Chapter-27/Section-B/Q-09

- Answer:

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Given limit $= \lim _{x \rightarrow \frac{\pi}{6}} \frac{(2 \sin x-1)(\sin x+1)}{(2 \sin x-1)(\sin x-1)} = \lim _{x \rightarrow \frac{\pi}{6}} \frac{(\sin x+1)}{(\sin x-1)}$
