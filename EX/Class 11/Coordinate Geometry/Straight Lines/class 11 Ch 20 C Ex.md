### Answer to Question 14

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-3/Chapter-20/Section-C/Q-14

- Answer:

$5 x-y+20=0$

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Slope of $A B=m_{1}=\frac{6-5}{-3-2}=\frac{-1}{5}$.
> 
> Now, $m_{1} m_{2}=-1 \Leftrightarrow \frac{-1}{5} \times m_{2}=-1 \Leftrightarrow m_{2}=5$.
> 
> $\therefore \quad$ required equation is $\frac{y-5}{x+3}=5 \Leftrightarrow 5 x-y+20=0$.
>
