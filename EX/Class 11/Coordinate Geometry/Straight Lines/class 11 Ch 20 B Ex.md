> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Slope of $A B=$ slope of $B C$
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> \Rightarrow \quad \frac{\left(y_{1}-k\right)}{\left(x_{1}-h\right)}=\frac{\left(y_{2}-y_{1}\right)}{\left(x_{2}-x_{1}\right)} & \Rightarrow \frac{\left(k-y_{1}\right)}{\left(h-x_{1}\right)}=\frac{\left(y_{2}-y_{1}\right)}{\left(x_{2}-x_{1}\right)} \\
> & \Rightarrow\left(h-x_{1}\right)\left(y_{2}-y_{1}\right)=\left(k-y_{1}\right)\left(x_{2}-x_{1}\right) .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
