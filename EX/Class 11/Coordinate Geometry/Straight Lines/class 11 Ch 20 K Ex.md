### Answer to Question 08

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-3/Chapter-20/Section-K/Q-08

- Answer:

$x=1$

> [!INFO] Hint
> 
> Let the required line be
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & \quad(3 x-4 y+1)+k(5 x+y-1)=0 \\
> \Rightarrow & \quad(3+5 k) x+(k-4) y+(1-k)=0 \\
> \Rightarrow & \quad(3+5 k) x+(k-4) y=(k-1) \\
> \Rightarrow & \quad \frac{(3+5 k)}{(k-1)} x+\frac{(k-4)}{(k-1)} y=0 \Rightarrow \frac{x}{\frac{(k-1)}{(3+5 k)}}+\frac{y}{\frac{(k-1)}{(k-4)}}=1 \\
> \therefore & \quad \frac{k-1}{3+5 k}=\frac{k-1}{k-4} \Rightarrow k-4=3+5 k \Rightarrow 4 k=-7 \Rightarrow k=\frac{-7}{4} .
> \end{aligned}
> $$
>
