### Answer to Question 11

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-1/Chapter-03/Section-F/Q-11

- Answer:

$\operatorname{dom}(f)=R$, range $(f)=[0,1)$

> [!INFO] Hint
>
> $x-[x]=0$ for $x \in Z$ and $x-[x]>0$ for $x \in R-Z$.
> $\therefore \quad x-[x] \geq 0$ for $x \in R$. So, $\operatorname{dom}(f)=R$.
> Also, $0 \leq x-[x]<1 \Rightarrow 0 \leq \sqrt{x-[x]}<1$. So, range $(f)=[0,1)$.
>
>

### Answer to Question 15

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-11/Unit-1/Chapter-03/Section-F/Q-15

- Answer:

$k=-1$

> [!INFO] Hint
>
> $f(f(x))=f\left(\frac{k x}{x+1}\right)=\frac{k \times \frac{k x}{x+1}}{\frac{k x}{x+1}+1}=\frac{k^{2} x}{k x+x+1}$. So, $\frac{k^{2} x}{k x+x+1}=x$.
> $\therefore \quad k^{2}=k x+x+1 \Rightarrow k^{2}-k x-(x+1)=0 \Rightarrow k=\frac{x \pm \sqrt{x^{2}+4(x+1)}}{2}=\frac{x \pm(x+2)}{2}$
> $\Rightarrow \quad k=x+1$ or $k=-1 \Rightarrow k=-1 \quad[\because k$ is a constant $]$.
>
>
