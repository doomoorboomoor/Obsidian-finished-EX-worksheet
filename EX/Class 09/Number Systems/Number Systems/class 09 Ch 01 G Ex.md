### Answer to Question 17.

> [!INFO]
>
> $\frac{9^{n} \times 3^{2} \times\left(3^{-\frac{n}{2}}\right)^{-2}-(27)^{n}}{3^{3m} \times 2^{3}}=\frac{1}{27}$
> $\Leftrightarrow \quad \frac{\left(3^{2}\right)^{n} \times 3^{2} \times 3^{\left(-\frac{n}{2}\right) \times(-2)}-\left(3^{3}\right)^{n}}{3^{3m} \times 8}=\frac{1}{27}$
> 
> $$
> \begin{aligned}

> & \Leftrightarrow \quad 3n-3m=-3 \Leftrightarrow n-m=-1 \Leftrightarrow m-n=1 .
> \end{aligned}
