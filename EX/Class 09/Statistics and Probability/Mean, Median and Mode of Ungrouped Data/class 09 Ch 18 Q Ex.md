### Answer to Question 09.

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-09/Unit-6/Chapter-18/Section-Q/Q-09

- Answer:

(b)

> [!INFO]
>
> $\bar{z}=\frac{\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\ldots+x_{n}\right)+\left(y_{1}+y_{2}+\ldots+y_{n}\right)}{2n}$.
>
> But, $\bar{x}=\frac{x_{1}+x_{2}+\ldots+x_{n}}{n} \Rightarrow\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\ldots+x_{n}\right)=n \bar{x}$.
> And, $\bar{y}=\frac{y_{1}+y_{2}+\ldots+y_{n}}{n} \Rightarrow\left(y_{1}+y_{2}+\ldots+y_{n}\right)=n \bar{y}$.
> $\therefore \quad \bar{z}=\frac{n \bar{x}+n \bar{y}}{2n}=\frac{n(\bar{x}+\bar{y})}{2n}=\frac{(\bar{x}+\bar{y})}{2}$.
