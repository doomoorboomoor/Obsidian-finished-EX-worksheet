### Answer to Question 02.

- [ ] InBoX/Ans/Ex/Class-09/Unit-5/Chapter-14/Section-A/Q-02

- Answer:

$900 \mathrm{~m}, 300 \mathrm{~m}$

> [!INFO]
>
> Let the altitude be $x$ metres. Then, base $=3x$ metres.
> $\therefore \quad$ area $=\left(\frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{altitude}\right)=\left(\frac{1}{2} \times 3x \times x\right) \mathrm{m}^{2}=\frac{3x^{2}}{2} \mathrm{m}^{2}$.
> But, area $=\frac{\text{total cost of sowing}}{\text{rate per hectare}}=\frac{783}{58}$ hectares $=\left(\frac{27}{2} \times 10000\right) \mathrm{m}^{2}=135000 \mathrm{m}^{2}$.
> $\therefore \quad \frac{3x^{2}}{2}=135000 \Rightarrow x^{2}=90000 \Rightarrow x=300$.
>
