> New edge $=(150 \% \text{ of } a) \mathrm{cm}=\left(\frac{150 a}{100}\right) \mathrm{cm}=\frac{3a}{2} \mathrm{~cm}$.
> New surface area $=\left\{6 \times\left(\frac{3a}{2}\right)^{2}\right\} \mathrm{cm}^{2}=\frac{27a^{2}}{2} \mathrm{~cm}^{2}$.
> 
> Surface area increased $=\left(\frac{27a^{2}}{2}-6a^{2}\right) \mathrm{cm}^{2}=\left(\frac{15a^{2}}{2}\right) \mathrm{cm}^{2}$.
> Percentage increase in surface area $=\left(\frac{15a^{2}}{2} \times \frac{1}{6a^{2}} \times 100\right) \%=125 \%$.
>
